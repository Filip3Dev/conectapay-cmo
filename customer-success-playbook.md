## Executive Summary

This playbook defines the proactive customer success methodology for ConectaPay. Our mission is to ensure every customer achieves their desired outcomes through WhatsApp automation and Pix payment recovery, driving retention, expansion, and advocacy.

**Core Philosophy**: Proactive > Reactive. We identify risks and opportunities before they become problems or missed revenue.

**Key Objectives**:
- Reduce churn to <5% monthly
- Achieve 20% expansion revenue (upsell/cross-sell)
- Generate 40% of new leads from customer referrals
- Maintain 70+ NPS
- Reach 90% customer health score (average)

## 1. Customer Lifecycle Stages

### 1.1 Lifecycle Overview

```
ONBOARDING → ADOPTION → EXPANSION → ADVOCACY → RENEWAL
   (14 days)   (90 days)   (Ongoing)   (Ongoing)   (Annual)
```

### 1.2 Stage Definitions

#### **Stage 1: Onboarding (Days 1-14)**

**Objective**: Customer achieves first value moment (FVM) - recovers first abandoned sale via WhatsApp.

**Success Criteria**:
- Account setup complete (WhatsApp Business API connected)
- Payment method configured (Pix key)
- First automation campaign created
- First recovered sale transaction

**Key Activities**:
| Day | Activity | Owner |
|-----|----------|-------|
| 1 | Welcome call + account setup | CSM |
| 1-3 | WhatsApp integration | Technical |
| 3-7 | First campaign creation | CSM |
| 7-10 | First recovered sale milestone | CSM |
| 14 | Onboarding completion review | CSM |

**Red Flags**:
- No integration completed by Day 3
- No campaign created by Day 7
- No recovered sale by Day 14

---

#### **Stage 2: Adoption (Days 15-90)**

**Objective**: Customer integrates ConectaPay into daily operations and sees consistent ROI.

**Success Criteria**:
- 20+ automated messages sent weekly
- 10+ sales recovered per month
- Customer uses 3+ core features
- NPS survey completed with score 7+

**Key Activities**:
| Week | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 2 | Feature training (advanced features) | CSM |
| 3 | Workflow optimization session | CSM |
| 4 | ROI review (show recovered revenue) | CSM |
| 6 | Best practices sharing | CSM |
| 8 | Feature adoption check | CSM |
| 12 | 90-day success review | CSM |

**Red Flags**:
- <10 automated messages/week
- Zero recovered sales in 30-day window
- Login frequency <1x/week
- Support tickets increasing without resolution

---

#### **Stage 3: Expansion (Day 91+)**

**Objective**: Customer grows with ConectaPay through upsell, cross-sell, and increased usage.

**Success Criteria**:
- Expands to higher tier (20% of customers)
- Adds new features/modules (30% of customers)
- Increases monthly automation volume by 50%

**Expansion Triggers**:
| Trigger | Indicator | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Volume cap | Approaching message limit | Offer tier upgrade |
| Feature request | Asks for premium feature | Present upgrade path |
| New location | Opens second business unit | Multi-location package |
| Team growth | Adds staff members | Additional seats |

**Expansion Playbook**:
1. Identify trigger via health monitoring
2. Prepare ROI case study from their account
3. Schedule expansion call (15 min)
4. Present tier comparison with business case
5. Offer limited-time incentive (10% discount annual)

---

#### **Stage 4: Advocacy (Ongoing)**

**Objective**: Customer becomes ConectaPay promoter, driving referrals and case studies.

**Success Criteria**:
- NPS score 9-10 (promoter)
- Refers 2+ new customers
- Participates in case study or testimonial
- Speaks at webinar or event

**Advocacy Tiers**:

| Tier | Criteria | Rewards |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Bronze** | NPS 9-10, 1 referral | R$100 account credit |
| **Silver** | NPS 9-10, 3 referrals, testimonial | R$300 credit + feature priority |
| **Gold** | NPS 9-10, 5+ referrals, case study | R$500 credit + free year + beta access |

---

#### **Stage 5: Renewal (Annual)**

**Objective**: Secure contract renewal with price increase or expansion.

**Success Criteria**:
- 95%+ renewal rate
- 80% of renewals with price increase
- 60% of renewals with expansion

**Renewal Timeline**:

| Days Before Renewal | Activity |
|---------------------|----------|
| 90 | Renewal risk assessment (health score review) |
| 60 | QBR meeting + renewal proposal |
| 30 | Finalize renewal terms + address objections |
| 14 | Send renewal invoice/contract |
| 7 | Payment reminder (if unpaid) |
| 0 | Renewal date |

**At-Risk Renewal Protocol**:
- Health score <50: Escalate to CS Director
- Usage decline >30%: Deep dive call + win-back campaign
- Competitor mentioned: Executive sponsor involvement + discount offer

## 2. Health Scoring Methodology

### 2.1 Health Score Formula

**Overall Health Score** (0-100) = Weighted Average:

| Component | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Product Usage** | 35% | Login frequency, features used, automation volume |
| ** Engagement** | 25% | Response rate, meeting attendance, email opens |
| **Outcome Achievement** | 20% | Recovered sales, ROI achieved, goals met |
| **Sentiment** | 20% | NPS score, support ticket tone, survey feedback |

**Health Tiers**:
- **90-100**: 🟢 **Healthy** - Advocate, expansion candidate
- **70-89**: 🟢 **Good** - On track, monitor for growth
- **50-69**: 🟡 **At Risk** - Intervention required
- **30-49**: 🟠 **Critical** - Red account, immediate action
- **0-29**: 🔴 **Churn Imminent** - Last chance campaign

---

### 2.2 Component Scoring Details

#### **Product Usage (35 points)**

| Metric | Scoring |
|--------|---------|
| Login frequency | Daily (10), Weekly (7), Bi-weekly (4), Monthly (2), <Monthly (0) |
| Automation volume | >100/week (10), 50-99/week (7), 20-49/week (4), <20/week (0) |
| Features used | 5+ (10), 3-4 (7), 2 (4), 1 (0) |
| Data integration | 3+ sources (5), 2 sources (3), 1 source (1), none (0) |

#### **Engagement (25 points)**

| Metric | Scoring |
|--------|---------|
| Email open rate | >70% (10), 50-70% (7), 30-49% (4), <30% (0) |
| Meeting attendance | 90%+ (10), 70-89% (7), 50-69% (4), <50% (0) |
| Response time | <24h (5), 24-48h (3), 48-72h (1), >72h (0) |

#### **Outcome Achievement (20 points)**

| Metric | Scoring |
|--------|---------|
| Recovered sales/month | >20 (10), 10-19 (7), 5-9 (4), <5 (0) |
| ROI achieved | >300% (5), 200-299% (3), 100-199% (1), <100% (0) |
| Goal attainment | All goals (5), Most goals (3), Some goals (1), None (0) |

#### **Sentiment (20 points)**

| Metric | Scoring |
|--------|---------|
| NPS score | 9-10 (10), 7-8 (7), 5-6 (3), 0-4 (0) |
| Support ticket tone | Positive (5), Neutral (3), Negative (0) |
| Survey feedback | Very positive (5), Positive (3), Mixed (1), Negative (0) |

## 4. Proactive Intervention Playbooks

### 4.1 At-Risk Customer Playbook (Health Score 50-69)

**Trigger**: Health score drops to 50-69

**Timeline**: 14-day intervention cycle

**Step 1: Day 1 - Diagnosis**
- Send health score decline email
- Schedule 30-minute discovery call
- Review usage data + support history
- Identify root cause (technical, value, staffing)

**Step 2: Day 3 - Action Plan**
- Present recovery plan with timeline
- Assign dedicated support (if needed)
- Provide quick-win resources (templates, guides)
- Set weekly check-ins for 4 weeks

**Step 3: Day 7 - Progress Check**
- Review usage metrics improvement
- Address new blockers
- Adjust plan if needed
- Celebrate small wins

**Step 4: Day 14 - Evaluation**
- Reassess health score
- If improved → Transition to monitoring
- If unchanged → Escalate to CS Director
- If declined → Red account protocol

---

### 4.2 Red Account Playbook (Health Score 30-49)

**Trigger**: Health score drops to 30-49 OR high-risk signal

**Timeline**: 7-day intensive intervention

**Day 1: Executive Engagement**
- CS Director + CSM joint call
- Understand pain points + validate urgency
- Offer immediate concessions (discount, extended trial)
- Assign success manager (if not already)

**Day 2: Technical Deep Dive**
- Technical lead reviews integration
- Identify bugs or friction points
- Create hotfix timeline (if applicable)
- Provide workaround solutions

**Day 3: Value Reinforcement**
- Calculate ROI achieved to date
- Share relevant case study
- Offer strategic consultation
- Co-create success plan

**Day 5: Decision Point**
- Confirm commitment or churn risk
- If staying → Reinforce support plan
- If leaving → Win-back campaign (see 4.5)

**Day 7: Final Review**
- Health score reassessment
- If no improvement → Accept churn + conduct exit interview

---

### 4.3 New Customer Playbook (First 90 Days)

**See detailed framework**: `customer-onboarding-framework.md`

**Key Proactive Touchpoints**:
- Day 1: Welcome + setup
- Day 7: First value milestone
- Day 30: Adoption check
- Day 60: Optimization session
- Day 90: Success review

---

### 4.4 Expansion Playbook (Health Score 70+)

**Trigger**: Expansion signal identified

**Step 1: Preparation**
- Pull customer's ROI metrics
- Identify relevant case study
- Prepare tier comparison document
- Calculate expansion ROI projection

**Step 2: Outreach**
- Send "growth opportunity" email
- Schedule 15-minute expansion call
- Include personalized ROI snapshot

**Step 3: Presentation**
- Show current impact
- Present expansion path
- Offer limited-time incentive
- Handle objections

**Step 4: Closing**
- Send proposal within 24 hours
- Follow up in 3 days
- Offer trial for premium features
- Close within 14 days

---

### 4.5 Win-Back Playbook (Churned Customers)

**Trigger**: Customer cancels or churns

**Timing**: 30, 60, 90 days post-churn

**30-Day Win-Back**:
- Personal email from founder
- "We miss you" + 3-months 50% discount
- Ask for feedback + offer to address
- Response target: 15% reply rate

**60-Day Win-Back**:
- Product update email (new features)
- "What's new at ConectaPay"
- 25% discount for 6 months
- Response target: 10% reply rate

**90-Day Win-Back**:
- Final outreach from CS Director
- "Last call" + lifetime discount (20%)
- Request exit interview (if declining)
- Response target: 5% reply rate

**Success Metrics**:
- 10% win-back rate (overall)
- 60-day reactivation timeline
- 90% retention of won-back customers

## 6. QBR Framework

### 6.1 QBR Purpose & Goals

**Purpose**: Strategic review to align ConectaPay value with customer business outcomes.

**Goals**:
1. Review ROI achieved to date
2. Identify expansion opportunities
3. Align on upcoming goals
4. Strengthen relationship
5. Gather strategic feedback

---

### 6.2 QBR Cadence

| Customer Tier | Frequency | Duration | Attendees |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Enterprise (R$5k+/mo) | Quarterly | 60 min | CSM, Customer Decision Maker |
| Mid-Market (R$1-5k/mo) | Semi-annually | 45 min | CSM, Customer Manager |
| SMB (<R$1k/mo) | Annually | 30 min | CSM, Customer Owner |

---

### 6.3 QBR Agenda Template

**Pre-QBR Preparation** (CSM):
1. Pull customer metrics (90 days)
2. Calculate ROI achieved
3. Identify expansion opportunities
4. Prepare slide deck
5. Send pre-meeting materials (3 days prior)

---

**QBR Meeting Agenda**:

| Time | Section | Content |
|------|---------|---------|
| 5 min | Welcome & Relationship Check | Personal connection, business updates |
| 10 min | Executive Summary | Key wins, metrics snapshot, ROI achieved |
| 10 min | Deep Dive: Success Stories | Recovered sales, automation highlights, testimonials |
| 10 min | Goals Review | Previous goals status, achievements, gaps |
| 10 min | Strategic Alignment | Upcoming business goals, ConectaFit fit |
| 10 min | Expansion Opportunities | New features, tier upgrade, additional locations |
| 5 min | Feedback & Next Steps | Questions, concerns, action items, next QBR date |

---

### 6.4 QBR Slide Deck Outline (10 slides)

1. **Title Slide**: Customer name, date, Q2 2026 QBR
2. **Executive Summary**: 3 key wins, ROI snapshot, health score
3. **Impact Dashboard**: Recovered sales, automation volume, response rates
4. **Success Stories**: 2-3 specific examples with screenshots
5. **Goals Review**: Previous goals vs. actuals
6. **Strategic Alignment**: Customer goals for next quarter
7. **Expansion Opportunities**: Personalized recommendations
8. **Product Roadmap**: Upcoming features relevant to customer
9. **Feedback & Ask**: Customer input, next steps
10. **Thank You**: Contact info, next QBR date

---

### 6.5 Post-QBR Actions

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 24 hours | Send thank-you email + summary + action items |
| 7 days | Check on action item progress |
| 30 days | Mini-check-in on goals |
| 60 days | QBR preparation begins again |

### 9.2 Role Definitions

#### **Customer Success Manager (CSM)**

**Mission**: Own customer outcomes, drive retention and expansion.

**Responsibilities**:
- Onboard new customers (first 14 days)
- Conduct regular touchpoints (weekly/monthly/quarterly)
- Monitor health scores + intervene proactively
- Identify and close expansion opportunities
- Conduct QBRs
- Gather customer feedback + share with product

**Skills Required**:
- Portuguese fluency (native)
- English proficiency (B2+)
- 3+ years customer success or account management experience
- Experience with SMBs in Brazil
- Strong communication + negotiation skills
- Data-driven (comfortable with metrics, dashboards)
- Empathy + customer-centric mindset

**Compensation**:
- Base: R$8,000-10,000/month
- Bonus: 20% of salary (based on portfolio metrics)
- Commission: 5% of expansion revenue

---

#### **Customer Success Manager**

**Mission**: Lead CS team, strategy, and operations.

**Responsibilities**:
- Manage CS team (hiring, training, coaching)
- Own CS metrics (churn, expansion, NPS)
- Design and refine CS playbooks
- Cross-functional alignment (Sales, Product, Support)
- Escalation point for red accounts
- Forecast and report on CS business impact

**Skills Required**:
- 7+ years customer success or account management experience
- 3+ years people management
- Experience scaling CS team (1→10+ FTE)
- Strategic thinker + operational excellence
- Strong analytical + business acumen
- Executive presence (can talk to C-level at customers)

**Compensation**:
- Base: R$15,000-18,000/month
- Bonus: 30% of salary (based on company CS metrics)
- Equity: 0.1-0.2%

---

#### **Customer Success Operations**

**Mission**: Enable CS team through tools, processes, and data.

**Responsibilities**:
- Manage CS tech stack (CRM, health scoring, analytics)
- Create and maintain CS dashboards
- Automate CS workflows (email sequences, alerts)
- Analyze customer data + surface insights
- Support playbook execution and refinement
- Training and documentation

**Skills Required**:
- 2+ years operations or analytics experience
- Technical proficiency (CRM, automation tools, SQL basic)
- Data visualization (Tableau, Looker, or similar)
- Process optimization + documentation
- Strong communication (can translate data to insights)

**Compensation**:
- Base: R$7,000-9,000/month
- Bonus: 15% of salary (based on team productivity)

### B. Health Score Calculator (Excel Formula)

```
= (ProductUsageScore * 0.35) + (EngagementScore * 0.25) + (OutcomeScore * 0.20) + (SentimentScore * 0.20)
```

Where:
- `ProductUsageScore` = (LoginScore + AutomationScore + FeatureScore + IntegrationScore) / 35 * 100
- `EngagementScore` = (EmailOpenScore + MeetingScore + ResponseScore) / 25 * 100
- `OutcomeScore` = (RecoveredSalesScore + ROIScore + GoalScore) / 20 * 100
- `SentimentScore` = (NPSScore + SupportToneScore + SurveyScore) / 20 * 100
