# ConectaPay - Brand Voice and Messaging Guidelines

## Purpose
This document is the single source of truth for all ConectaPay brand communications. It ensures consistency, clarity, and effectiveness across every touchpoint with our audience.

**Use this document when:**
- Creating any marketing content (ads, social, email, landing pages)
- Writing customer communications (in-app messages, notifications, support)
- Developing sales scripts and outreach materials
- Onboarding new team members or contractors
- Reviewing content for brand alignment

---

## Brand Identity

### Who We Are
ConectaPay is a practical, results-driven platform that helps Brazilian SMBs recover lost revenue through WhatsApp automation and Pix payments. We're not a flashy tech company—we're a business solution that speaks the language of our customers.

### What We Believe
- **Revenue over features**: Every message should tie back to money saved or earned
- **Speed over perfection**: Quick implementation beats complex solutions
- **Real data over assumptions**: We use specific numbers, not vague promises
- **Simple over sophisticated**: Our audience values clarity over cleverness

### Our Promise
"Recupere receita perdida. Automatize follow-ups. Receba via Pix. Em 15 minutos."

## Brand Voice Pillars

### 1. Direct and Specific
We speak plainly, with specific numbers and clear outcomes. No fluff, no jargon, no ambiguity.

**✅ Right:**
"Recupere R$ 3.200 no primeiro mês"
"Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500+ todo mês com no-shows"
"Configure em 15 minutos. Funciona pra sempre."

**❌ Wrong:**
"Melhore sua eficiência operacional"
"Otimização de processos de agendamento"
"Solution-focused platform for growth"

### 2. Pain-Driven
We lead with the problem our audience faces, then show the cost of inaction, then offer the solution.

**✅ Right:**
"Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500+ todo mês com no-shows"
"Enquanto você faz ligações manuais, concorrentes recuperam R$ 3.200/mês"
"Você perdeu R$ 400 esta semana sem perceber"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Descubra uma nova forma de gerenciar sua clínica"
"Inovação em atendimento ao paciente"
"Transforme sua experiência com automação"

### 3. Urgent but Helpful
We create urgency through quantified loss, not artificial scarcity. We're solving a real problem that's costing money every day.

**✅ Right:**
"Enquanto você lê, perdeu R$ 50"
"Cada mês que passa = R$ 3.000 perdidos"
"Pare de perder dinheiro hoje"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Oferta limitada! Últimas vagas!"
"Só por hoje! Não perca!"
"Corra que está acabando!"

### 4. Brazilian and Local
We speak Portuguese as spoken in Brazilian businesses. We use WhatsApp and Pix as primary examples because that's what Brazilians use.

**✅ Right:**
"Envia lembretes no WhatsApp. Recebe via Pix. Pronto."
"99% dos brasileiros usam WhatsApp"
"Pagamento instantâneo via Pix"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Messaging platform with payment integration"
"Communication automation system"
"Multi-channel engagement tool"

### 5. Simple and Accessible
We avoid technical complexity. If a teenager couldn't understand it, we've made it too complicated.

**✅ Right:**
"Configure em 15 minutos"
"Se souber usar WhatsApp, sabe usar ConectaPay"
"5 minutos por dia (vs. 3 horas com ligações)"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Seamless API integration with omnichannel capabilities"
"Enterprise-grade infrastructure with scalable architecture"
"End-to-end encrypted workflow automation"

## Tone Spectrum

Our tone shifts based on context, but always stays within these bounds:

### Context 1: Problem Awareness (Pain-Focused)
**Tone**: Urgent, serious, eye-opening
**When**: First contact, problem-focused content, outreach

**Example:**
"Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500+ todo mês com no-shows. E o pior: você nem percebe. 2-3 pacientes faltam por dia. R$ 400-R$ 600 por semana. Dinheiro que já estava garantido e simplesmente some."

### Context 2: Solution Presentation (Benefit-Focused)
**Tone**: Confident, clear, reassuring
**When**: Explaining the product, landing pages, demos

**Example:**
"ConectaPay automatiza 100% dos lembretes via WhatsApp. Mensagem 24h antes. Confirmação em 1 clique. Pagamento antecipado via Pix (opcional). Clínicas como a sua recuperam R$ 3.200 no primeiro mês."

### Context 3: Social Proof (Validation-Focused)
**Tone**: Trustworthy, relatable, evidence-based
**When**: Case studies, testimonials, success stories

**Example:**
"Dra. Ana Paula, Clínica DermatoEssência (RJ): 'Perdíamos R$ 2.500/mês. Com automação, reduzimos para R$ 700. E ainda recebemos pagamento antecipado de 40% dos pacientes.' ROI de 6x no primeiro mês."

### Context 4: Objection Handling (Reassurance-Focused)
**Tone**: Patient, understanding, problem-solving
**When**: FAQs, sales conversations, support

**Example:**
"Não funciona com meu sistema? Integra com qualquer sistema (ClinicWeb, Doctoralia, Feegow) ou planilha simples. Configura em 15 minutos. Suporte via WhatsApp incluso."

### Context 5: Action and Urgency (Conversion-Focused)
**Tone**: Direct, confident, clear
**When**: CTAs, closing, follow-ups

**Example:**
"14 dias grátis. Cancele quando quiser. Configure em 15 minutos. Pare de perder dinheiro hoje."

## Language Guidelines

### Words to Use

**Revenue & Money:**
- Recuperar, recuperar, recuperar (not "ganhar" or "aumentar")
- R$ [specific amount]
- Perda, prejuízo, dinheiro que some
- ROI, retorno, payback

**Time & Effort:**
- 5 minutos por dia (not "pouco tempo")
- 3 horas/semana (not "muito tempo")
- Configure em 15 minutos (not "implementação rápida")
- Zero trabalho manual

**WhatsApp & Pix:**
- Lembrete automático via WhatsApp
- Confirmação em 1 clique
- Pagamento antecipado via Pix
- Instantâneo, sem taxas

**Results & Proof:**
- Clínicas como a sua
- Reduziu no-shows em 70%
- ROI de 10x no primeiro mês
- Antes/Depois comparisons

### Words to Avoid

**❌ Vague Business Jargon:**
- Otimizar, otimização → Use "Reduza tempo" or "Recupere dinheiro"
- Eficiência → Use specific time/money savings
- Escalável → Use "Funciona para clínicas de qualquer tamanho"
- Sinergia, leverage, disruptivo → Never use

**❌ Tech Slang:**
- Plataforma, solução → Use "ConectaPay" or "sistema"
- Integração → Use "Funciona com" or "Conecta com"
- Implementação → Use "Configuração"
- Dashboard, analytics → Use "Relatório" or "Acompanhamento"

**❌ Artificial Urgency:**
- Oferta limitada, última chance → Use quantified loss instead
- Exclusivo, VIP → Avoid entirely
- Só por hoje → Never use
- Não perca → Use "Pare de perder" instead

**❌ Overpromising:**
- Garantimos, 100% → Use "Clínicas como a sua recuperam..."
- Sempre, nunca → Use specific stats instead
- Melhor do mercado → Use "Reduz no-shows em 70%"

### Grammar and Style

**Sentences:**
- Keep sentences short (max 15 words)
- One idea per sentence
- Active voice, not passive
- Subject-verb-object order

**Numbers:**
- Always use R$ with amounts
- Use specific ranges: R$ 2.000-R$ 5.000
- Round to clean numbers: R$ 2.500 (not R$ 2.537)
- Use percentages: 70% (not "seventy percent")

**Formatting:**
- Use line breaks for emphasis
- Bullet points for lists
- Emojis sparingly (1-2 per post)
- ALL CAPS for short emphasis only (e.g., "ZERO work")

## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)

**Character Limits:**
- Primary Text: Max 125 characters for hook, expand below
- Headline: Max 40 characters
- Description: Max 30 characters

**Structure:**
1. Hook with pain + number (first 125 chars)
2. Problem breakdown (bullet points)
3. Solution introduction
4. Social proof or ROI
5. CTA

**Example:**
```
Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500+ todo mês com no-shows

❌ Horário vago que não pode ser preenchido
❌ Receita que não entra no caixa
❌ Equipe ociosa esperando

ConectaPay automatiza 100% dos lembretes via WhatsApp:
✅ Mensagem 24h antes
✅ Confirmação em 1 clique
✅ Pagamento antecipado via Pix

Clínicas como a sua recuperam R$ 3.200 no primeiro mês.

14 dias grátis. Cancele quando quiser.
```

**CTA Options:**
- "Começar Agora"
- "Testar 14 Dias Grátis"

### Email Marketing

**Subject Lines:**
- Max 50 characters
- Lead with pain or curiosity
- Include specific number when possible

**Examples:**
- "R$ 400 que sua clínica perdeu esta semana"
- "Sua secretária gasta 3 horas em ligações inúteis"
- "Clínicas que automatizam crescem 2x mais rápido"

**Structure:**
1. Hook with pain + number
2. Problem breakdown with bullets
3. Solution introduction
4. Social proof or case study
5. Clear CTA button
6. P.S. with urgency or bonus

**Best Practices:**
- Mobile-first (short paragraphs, max 3 lines)
- One CTA per email
- P.S. line with additional value
- Preview text matters (first 100 chars)

2. Context or research-based observation
3. Question (not pitch)

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Responding to "Too Expensive"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Actually, when you consider the ROI and the time savings, it's quite affordable. Plus we have different pricing tiers..."

**✅ Right:**
"Você investe R$ 297/mês para recuperar R$ 3.200/mês. ROI de 10x no primeiro mês. Em 30 dias você já lucrou R$ 2.900."

### Scenario 2: Responding to "Already Have a Solution"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Our solution is better because we use advanced AI and have more features and integrations..."

**✅ Right:**
"Entendo. Como funciona hoje sua taxa de confirmação? A maioria das clínicas que falo tem 60-70% com ligações manuais. Automação aumenta para 95%."

### Scenario 3: Responding to "Not Sure If Works for My Niche"

**❌ Wrong:**
"Our platform is highly flexible and can be customized for various industries and use cases..."

**✅ Right:**
"Funciona para qualquer negócio que agenda e recebe pagamento. Clínicas, dentistas, nutricionistas, psicólogos — todos recuperam R$ 2-5 mil/mês."

### Scenario 4: Introducing ConectaPay in Conversation

**❌ Wrong:**
"ConectaPay is an innovative SaaS platform that leverages WhatsApp automation and Pix payment integration to help SMBs reduce no-shows and recover revenue..."

**✅ Right:**
"ConectaPay automatiza lembretes via WhatsApp. Clínicas recuperam R$ 3.200/mês que perdiam com no-shows. Configure em 15 minutos."

### Scenario 5: Explaining ROI to Non-Technical Audience

**❌ Wrong:**
"The platform provides a comprehensive ROI calculation based on your current no-show rate, average consultation value, and weekly volume..."

**✅ Right:**
"Se você tem 10 no-shows por semana × R$ 200 = R$ 2.000 por semana. Isso é R$ 8.000 por mês perdidos. A gente recupera 70-80% disso."

## Brand Voice Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- **Lead with pain**: Start every message with the problem
- **Use specific numbers**: R$ amounts, percentages, timeframes
- **Keep it simple**: Short sentences, clear language
- **Include social proof**: Testimonials, case studies, stats
- **Create urgency**: Quantify cost of inaction
- **Speak Brazilian**: Use WhatsApp, Pix, local context
- **Focus on revenue**: Every message ties to money saved/earned
- **Be direct**: Say what you mean, no fluff
- **Use active voice**: "ConectaPay recupera" not "Revenue is recovered by"
- **Mobile-optimize**: Short paragraphs, line breaks, bullets

### ❌ DON'T

- **Use jargon**: "Synergy", "leverage", "optimization", "scalable"
- **Be vague**: "Improve efficiency", "save time", "grow faster"
- **Feature-dump**: List features without explaining benefits
- **Overpromise**: "100%", "always", "guarantee", "never"
- **Create artificial scarcity**: "Limited time", "exclusive", "VIP"
- **Write long paragraphs**: Max 3 lines, break often
- **Use passive voice**: "Mistakes were made" vs "We made a mistake"
- **Forget the CTA**: Every message needs a next step
- **Ignore mobile**: Long blocks of text don't get read
- **Be clever**: Clear beats clever every time

## Measuring Brand Voice Effectiveness

Track these metrics to ensure our voice resonates:

### Engagement Metrics
- **CTR**: 2.5%+ on ads (indicates strong hooks)
- **Engagement Rate**: 5%+ on organic posts (indicates resonance)
- **Response Rate**: 50%+ on outreach DMs (indicates personal tone)
- **Email Open Rate**: 25%+ (indicates strong subject lines)

### Conversion Metrics
- **Landing Page Conversion**: 40%+ (indicates clear messaging)
- **Lead Quality**: 70%+ qualified (indicates we attract right audience)
- **Sales Call Show Rate**: 80%+ (indicates we set right expectations)

### Brand Perception
- **Customer Feedback**: Do they quote our messaging back to us?
- **Referral Language**: Do customers describe us the way we describe ourselves?
- **Support Tickets**: Do users understand what we do before signing up?

### Iteration Process
1. **A/B test** different voice variations
2. **Track** metrics for 7-14 days
3. **Identify** winners and losers
4. **Document** learnings in this document
5. **Update** guidelines with validated language
