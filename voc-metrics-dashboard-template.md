## Alert Thresholds

### Real-Time Alerts (Slack/Discord/Email)

| Alert Type | Trigger | Action | Owner |
|------------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Churn Risk** | Sentiment negative + churn keywords | Immediate outreach | CS Lead |
| **Viral Negative** | Social post with 10K+ impressions, negative | <1 hour response | CMO |
| **NPS Drop** | NPS drops >10 points week-over-week | Investigation | VoC Lead |
| **Close-the-Loop Failure** | Item not closed in 30 days | Escalate | VoC Lead |
| **Feature Request Spike** | 10+ requests for same feature in 7 days | Prioritize | Product Lead |
| **Support Ticket Spike** | 50%+ increase in tickets | Investigate | CS Lead |
| **Review Bomb** | 10+ negative reviews in 24 hours | Crisis response | CMO + CEO |
| **CAB Resignation** | CAB member resigns | Exit interview | VoC Lead |
