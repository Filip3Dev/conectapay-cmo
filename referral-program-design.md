# ConectaPay Referral Program - Design & Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Program Name**: ConectaPay Indica (ConectaPay Refers)

**Objective**: Transform customers into advocates to drive viral growth and reduce CAC through a dual-sided incentive structure optimized for Brazilian SMBs.

**Target K-factor**: 0.4 → 0.8 (each customer brings 0.4-0.8 new customers)

**Expected Impact**:
- 30-40% of new growth from referrals (Year 1)
- CAC reduction: R$ 150 → R$ 75 (50% reduction on referred customers)
- 90-day activation: 25% of active customers referring

---

## Program Mechanics

### Core Structure: Dual-Sided Incentives

**Both referrer and referee receive rewards** - proven to drive 2-3x more referrals than one-sided programs.

| Role | Reward | Timing |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Referrer** (existing customer) | R$ 150 credit | After referee pays first invoice |
| **Referee** (new customer) | R$ 100 discount | Applied to first invoice |
| **Bonus** (top 10% referrers) | R$ 500/month | Monthly leaderboard |

### Why This Structure Works

1. **Dual-sided**: Eliminates social friction - both parties benefit
2. **Asymmetric**: Referrer gets more (R$ 150 vs R$ 100) - incentivizes action
3. **Delayed referrer payment**: Prevents fraud, ensures customer quality
4. **Leaderboard bonus**: Gamification drives super-referrers

## Program Economics

### Unit Economics Analysis

**Current CAC (Paid Ads)**: ~R$ 150/customer
**Referral CAC**: R$ 250 ÷ K-factor (0.4-0.8) = **R$ 75-125/customer**

**Break-Even Analysis**:
- If K-factor = 0.4: CAC = R$ 125 (17% savings vs paid)
- If K-factor = 0.8: CAC = R$ 75 (50% savings vs paid)

**LTV Impact**:
- Current LTV: ~R$ 1.500 (assuming R$ 300/month × 12 months × 40% margin)
- Max acquisition cost: R$ 750 (50% of LTV)
- Referral cost: R$ 250 (well within healthy range)

## Referral Mechanics

### How It Works: Customer Journey

#### Step 1: Discovery (Day 0-30)
**Trigger**: Customer activates account and completes first successful automation

**Action**:
- In-app referral prompt: "Ganhe R$ 150 por cada amigo que você indicar"
- WhatsApp message with referral link
- Email onboarding sequence includes referral CTA

**Assets**:
- Unique referral code: `conectapay.com.br/indica/[NOME]`
- QR code for physical locations (clinics, agencies)
- WhatsApp pre-filled message template

#### Step 2: Sharing (Ongoing)
**Channels**:
- WhatsApp share button (one-click send)
- LinkedIn post template
- Email template
- Instagram story template

**Message Template**:
```
"Olá [Nome]! Uso o ConectaPay para recuperar vendas
abandonadas no WhatsApp. É ótimo para [clínicas/imobiliárias].

Se você se cadastrar com meu link, ganha R$ 100 de desconto:
[LINK]

Eu também ganho R$ 150 quando você ativar a conta. 🎉"
```

#### Step 3: Conversion (Referee Journey)
1. Click referral link → Landing page with referrer info
2. Sign up with R$ 100 discount auto-applied
3. Complete onboarding
4. Run first automation
5. Pay first invoice (referee discount applied)
6. **Trigger**: Referrer receives R$ 150 credit notification

#### Step 4: Reward Distribution
- **Referee**: R$ 100 discount on first invoice (instant)
- **Referrer**: R$ 150 credit (after referee pays, ~30-45 days)
- **Credits**: Applied to next invoice, never expire

## Fraud Prevention Framework

### Risk Mitigation Strategies

#### 1. Self-Referral Detection
**Rule**: Same IP, device, or payment method = self-referral
**Action**: Flag for manual review, auto-reject with notification
**Technical**: Browser fingerprinting + payment method matching

#### 2. Quality Gate
**Rule**: Referee must pay first invoice before referrer gets credit
**Purpose**: Prevents fake accounts, ensures customer quality
**Timeline**: ~30-45 days from signup to reward

#### 3. Velocity Limits
**Rule**: Max 10 referrals/month without verification
**Action**: Manual outreach to high-volume referrers, partner program invitation
**Exception**: Approved partners (agencies, consultants) have higher limits

#### 4. Churn Protection
**Rule**: If referee churns within 90 days, clawback referrer credit
**Action**: Pro-rated credit recovery based on months active
**Grace period**: 90 days from referee activation

#### 5. Geographic Anomaly Detection
**Rule**: Sudden spike in referrals from unrelated regions
**Action**: Flag for review, require verification
**Example**: São Paulo customer suddenly referring 20 people in Manaus

### Fraud Detection Signals

| Signal | Risk Level | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Same IP/device | High | Auto-reject |
| Payment method match | High | Auto-reject |
| Referee churns < 30 days | Medium | Clawback credit |
| >10 referrals/month from new customer | Medium | Manual review |
| Referrers from unrelated geography | Low-Medium | Flag |

## Legal & Compliance

### Terms & Conditions

**Key Points**:
1. Rewards are account credits, not cash withdrawals
2. Referrer receives credit after referee pays first invoice
3. Credits never expire but are non-transferable
4. ConectaPay reserves right to cancel fraudulent referrals
5. Tiered rewards require manual approval for 25+ referrals/month

### Disclosure Requirements

**Brazilian Consumer Law**:
- Clear disclosure of reward terms
- No hidden conditions
- Easy opt-out from marketing communications
- Data privacy compliance (LGPD)

### Tax Implications

**Referrer Rewards**:
- Generally not taxable if < R$ 5.000/month
- Above threshold: Issue Recibo de Pagamento Autônomo (RPA)
- Consult accountant for specific cases

**Referee Discounts**:
- Treated as price reduction, not income
- No tax implications for customer

## Continuous Optimization

### A/B Testing Roadmap

**Test 1: Reward Amounts**
- Control: R$ 150 referrer / R$ 100 referee
- Variant: R$ 200 referrer / R$ 50 referee
- Metric: Referral volume, K-factor

**Test 2: Messaging Angle**
- Control: "Ganhe R$ 150" (gain-framed)
- Variant: "Pare de perder vendas" + referral offer (pain-framed)
- Metric: Click-through rate, conversion

**Test 3: Trigger Timing**
- Control: Day 30 first prompt
- Variant: Day 7 first prompt (after first automation)
- Metric: Participation rate, time-to-first-referral

**Test 4: Reward Delivery**
- Control: Credit after referee pays invoice
- Variant: Instant R$ 50 + R$ 100 after payment
- Metric: Referrer satisfaction, fraud rate
