## Best Practices for Close-the-Loop Emails

### DO's ✅

1. **Personalize Always**
   - Use customer name
   - Reference specific feedback
   - Show you listened

2. **Be Quick**
   - Positive feedback: Within 7 days
   - Negative feedback: Within 24 hours
   - Feature requests: Within 7 days
   - Bug reports: Within 24 hours (acknowledge)

3. **Be Human**
   - Sign with real name (not "ConectaPay Team")
   - Use conversational language
   - Show empathy

4. **Offer Next Steps**
   - Always include CTA or next action
   - Make it easy to respond
   - Provide multiple channels (email, WhatsApp, phone)

5. **Close the Loop**
   - Notify when action is complete
   - Explain what changed
   - Thank them again

### DON'Ts ❌

1. **Don't Be Generic**
   - ❌ "Thanks for your feedback"
   - ✅ "Thanks for suggesting [Feature]"

2. **Don't Over-Promise**
   - ❌ "We'll build this soon" (if not true)
   - ✅ "We'll evaluate this for our roadmap"

3. **Don't Be Defensive**
   - ❌ "Actually, the feature works if you..."
   - ✅ "Sorry it wasn't clear. Let me help."

4. **Don't Ignore Negative Feedback**
   - ❌ No response
   - ✅ Reach out within 24 hours

5. **Don't Spam**
   - Minimum 30 days between major surveys
   - Don't send same customer multiple emails per week

## Email Subject Line Best Practices

### Good Subject Lines ✅

- "Você pediu, nós fizemos! 🚀 [Feature Name]" (Curiosity + benefit)
- "Sobre seu feedback - Queremos ajudar" (Empathetic)
- "Problema corrigido - [Bug Name]" (Clear action)
- "Obrigado, [Nome]! 🎉" (Personal + celebration)
- "Uma atualização - Você mencionou [Issue]" (Personalized)

### Bad Subject Lines ❌

- "Update from ConectaPay" (Too generic)
- "Feedback received" (Boring, robotic)
- "Newsletter #42" (Impersonal)
- "Important information" (Vague, clickbaity)
- "Hello" (No context)
