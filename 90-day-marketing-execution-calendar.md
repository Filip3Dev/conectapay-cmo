## Executive Summary

This 90-day execution calendar translates ConectaPay's comprehensive marketing strategy into a day-by-day action plan. It integrates all strategic frameworks (smoke tests, ICP discovery, ABM, content, partnerships) into a unified execution roadmap with clear owners, deliverables, and success metrics.

**Primary Objective**: Launch smoke tests, secure 15-20 customer interviews, validate ICP demand, and build predictable lead generation.

**Key Success Metrics** (Day 90):
- 15-20 customer interviews completed
- 500+ waitlist signups
- R$ 50K+ smoke test budget deployed
- 3+ validated ICP segments
- 500+ qualified leads generated
- R$ 150-250 CPL (cost per lead)

## Risk Management

### Risk 1: Low Conversion Rate (<10%)
**Probability**: Medium (30%)
**Impact**: High (waste ad spend)
**Mitigation**:
- [ ] Pre-test with small budget (R$ 500)
- [ ] A/B test landing page elements
- [ ] Test different value propositions
- [ ] Test different CTAs (waitlist vs demo vs trial)
- [ ] Pivot messaging if <10% after R$ 2.000 spend

### Risk 2: High CPL (>R$ 100)
**Probability**: Medium (25%)
**Impact**: High (not scalable)
**Mitigation**:
- [ ] Narrow audience targeting
- [ ] Improve ad quality score (relevance)
- [ ] Test cheaper channels (organic, partnerships)
- [ ] Focus on high-LTV segments (clínicas over academias)
- [ ] Improve landing page conversion (better quality leads)

### Risk 3: Low Interview Response Rate (<5%)
**Probability**: Medium (20%)
**Impact**: Medium (no ICP validation)
**Mitigation**:
- [ ] Improve outreach personalization
- [ ] Offer incentive (R$ 200 gift card)
- [ ] Use founder/CTO for outreach (more credible)
- [ ] Test different channels (email vs DM vs phone)
- [ ] Partner with industry associations (endorsement)

### Risk 4: Ad Account Bans
**Probability**: Low (10%)
**Impact**: Critical (campaign stops)
**Mitigation**:
- [ ] Follow Meta/Google ad policies strictly
- [ ] Avoid restricted claims (guarantees, before/after)
- [ ] Use compliant landing page (privacy policy, terms)
- [ ] Diversify channels (don't rely on 1 platform)
- [ ] Build organic audience (email list, social)

### Risk 5: No Product-Market Fit
**Probability**: Medium (40%)
**Impact**: Critical (business fails)
**Mitigation**:
- [ ] Talk to 15-20 customers before building
- [ ] Test demand before coding (smoke test)
- [ ] Be willing to pivot (segment, problem, solution)
- [ ] Run fast experiments (fail fast, learn faster)
- [ ] Focus on pain, not product (problem-first)

## Decision Framework: Go/No-Go at Day 45

### Go (Continue to Phase 3) IF:
- [ ] CPL < R$ 50 (affordable)
- [ ] Conversion rate > 12% (decent interest)
- [ ] 5+ interviews completed (learning happening)
- [ ] 1+ segments showing strong response (ICP signal)
- [ ] Team has capacity to execute Phase 3

### No-Go (Pivot or Stop) IF:
- [ ] CPL > R$ 100 (too expensive)
- [ ] Conversion rate < 8% (no interest)
- [ ] <3 interviews completed (no access)
- [ ] All segments weak response (no ICP fit)
- [ ] Team burned out or blocked

### Pivot (Change Strategy) IF:
- [ ] CPL R$ 50-100 (expensive but workable)
- [ ] Conversion 8-12% (moderate interest)
- [ ] 3-5 interviews completed (some learning)
- [ ] 1 segment better than others (partial fit)

**Pivot Options**:
1. Change target audience (new segment)
2. Change value proposition (new messaging)
3. Change offer (different pricing/feature)
4. Change channel (new acquisition strategy)
5. Change problem (different pain point)
