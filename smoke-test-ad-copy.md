## A/B Testing Framework

### Test Matrix

| Test | Variable | Variation A | Variation B | Metric |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Headline | Pain-driven | Time-focused | CTR |
| 2 | Creative | Image | Video | CTR |
| 3 | CTA | "Começar Agora" | "14 Dias Grátis" | Conversion |
| 4 | Social Proof | No testimonial | With testimonial | Conversion |
| 5 | Pricing | Show price | Hide price | Lead quality |

### Success Metrics

**Click-Through Rate (CTR)**:
- Target: 2.5%+
- Good: 3.5%+
- Excellent: 5%+

**Cost Per Click (CPC)**:
- Target: R$ 1.50-2.50
- Good: R$ 1.00-1.50
- Excellent: <R$ 1.00

**Cost Per Lead (CPL)**:
- Target: R$ 15-25
- Good: R$ 10-15
- Excellent: <R$ 10

**Landing Page Conversion**:
- Target: 40%+
- Good: 50%+
- Excellent: 60%+

## Notes for Execution

- Always run ads in pairs (A/B test)
- Let each test run minimum 7 days
- Track micro-conversions (button clicks, form views)
- Retarget website visitors with different messaging
- Use lookalike audiences after 100+ leads
- Test on both Facebook and Instagram separately
- Monitor quality score (affects CPC significantly)
