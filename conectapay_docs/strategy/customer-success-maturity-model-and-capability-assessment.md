## Part 1: Maturity Level Framework

### Overview

The ConectaPay CS Maturity Model defines 5 levels of organizational maturity, adapted from CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration) and tailored for B2B SaaS CS operations in the Brazilian SMB market.

**Maturity Level Formula**:
```
Overall Maturity = Average(Domain Scores) × Implementation Weight
where Implementation Weight = % of documented capabilities actually operational
```

**Current Calculation (April 2026)**:
- Average Domain Score: 1.8/5.0 (extensive planning exists)
- Implementation Weight: 0.30 (30.5% of critical infrastructure operational)
- **Overall Maturity: 1.2/5.0** (Ad-Hoc with foundational documentation)

## Part 2: Capability Assessment Framework

### Overview

The CS Capability Assessment evaluates 8 critical domains that determine CS organizational maturity. Each domain is scored 1-5 across 4 dimensions:

**Scoring Dimensions**:
1. **Process Maturity**: How well-defined and documented is the capability?
2. **Implementation**: How consistently is it executed in practice?
3. **Measurement**: Is it measured with clear KPIs and targets?
4. **Tooling**: Are tools/systems in place to support it?

**Domain Score Formula**:
```
Domain Score = (Process + Implementation + Measurement + Tooling) / 4
```

### Appendix D: Glossary

**Activation Rate**: Percentage of customers who complete onboarding and achieve first value

**Advocate**: Customer who actively promotes ConectaPay (references, case studies, referrals)

**Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers who cancel in a given period

**CLV (Customer Lifetime Value)**: Total revenue expected from a customer over their relationship

**CSAT (Customer Satisfaction)**: Measure of customer satisfaction with support interactions

**CSM (Customer Success Manager)**: Role responsible for customer health and retention

**Health Score**: Composite score (0-100) indicating customer health and churn risk

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**: Definition of the perfect customer for ConectaPay

**NPS (Net Promoter Score)**: Measure of customer loyalty and likelihood to recommend

**NRR (Net Revenue Retention)**: Revenue retention including expansion and churn

**Onboarding**: Process of guiding new customers from signup to first value

**Playbook**: Standardized process for handling specific customer scenarios

**QBR (Quarterly Business Review)**: Strategic meeting with key customers to review value and goals

**SLA (Service Level Agreement)**: Commitment to response/resolution time for support

**Time-to-First-Value (TTFV)**: Time from signup to customer achieving first value

**VoC (Voice of Customer)**: Systematic collection and analysis of customer feedback
