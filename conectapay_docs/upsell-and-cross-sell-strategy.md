# ConectaPay Upsell and Cross-sell Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Objective**: Maximize revenue per customer through strategic expansion, driving Net Revenue Retention (NRR) to 110%+.

**Target Metrics**:
- **Expansion Revenue**: 20% of total MRR (Month 12)
- **Upsell Rate**: 15% of customers upgrade annually
- **Cross-sell Penetration**: 30% adopt add-ons
- **Tier Migration**: 10% move up tiers annually
- **NRR**: 110%+ (expansion offsets churn)

**Economic Impact**:
- At 500 customers, 20% expansion = **R$ 30.000/month MRR** (R$ 360.000/year)
- At 1.000 customers, 20% expansion = **R$ 60.000/month MRR** (R$ 720.000/year)
- Expansion has 90%+ gross margin (pure profit after fixed costs)

---

## Part 1: Expansion Opportunity Analysis

### Customer Lifecycle Expansion Map

**Phase 1: Onboarding (Day 0-30)**
- **Goal**: Successful first value achievement
- **Expansion Opportunity**: None (focus on activation)
- **Risk**: Early churn if upsold too early

**Phase 2: Adoption (Day 31-90)**
- **Goal**: Product adoption, habit formation
- **Expansion Opportunity**: Feature add-ons
- **Readiness Signals**:
  - 10+ automations running
  - R$ 1.000+ in sales recovered
  - Daily/weekly usage

**Phase 3: Growth (Day 91-180)**
- **Goal**: Scaling results, team adoption
- **Expansion Opportunity**: Tier upgrade, additional seats
- **Readiness Signals**:
  - Hitting plan limits (messages, automations)
  - Team members requesting access
  - R$ 3.000+ recovered monthly
  - Asking for advanced features

**Phase 4: Maturity (Day 181+)**
- **Goal**: Optimization, integration with business
- **Expansion Opportunity**: Enterprise features, multi-location
- **Readiness Signals**:
  - Multiple locations/business units
  - Integration with other systems
  - Custom workflow requirements
  - Strategic partnership interest

---

### Expansion Readiness Scoring (0-100)

**Score Components**:

| Component | Weight | Scoring Logic |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Usage Intensity** | 30 points | 30 = Daily, 20 = Weekly, 10 = Monthly, 0 = Rarely |
| **Value Realized** | 25 points | 25 = R$ 5K+ recovered, 15 = R$ 2-5K, 5 = R$ 500-2K, 0 = <R$ 500 |
| **Plan Limit Proximity** | 20 points | 20 = At 80%+ limit, 10 = 50-80%, 0 = <50% |
| **Tenure** | 15 points | 15 = 6+ months, 10 = 3-6 months, 5 = 1-3 months, 0 = <1 month |
| **Engagement** | 10 points | 10 = High (NPS 9-10, asks questions), 5 = Medium, 0 = Low |

**Classification**:
- **80-100**: Expansion Ready → Immediate outreach
- **60-79**: Warm → Nurture, check in 30 days
- **40-59**: Cold → Focus on adoption first
- **0-39**: At Risk → Retention focus, not expansion

## Part 4: Timing & Outreach Strategy

### When to Upsell (Customer Journey)

| Month | Focus | Upsell Readiness | Action |
|-------|-------|-------------------|--------|
| **Month 1** | Activation | 0% | No upsell (focus on success) |
| **Month 2** | Adoption | 0% | No upsell (building habits) |
| **Month 3** | Value Demonstration | 10% | Soft signals only |
| **Month 4** | Expansion Signals | 40% | **Start upsell outreach** |
| **Month 6** | Growth Phase | 70% | **Peak upsell timing** |
| **Month 9** | Maturity | 60% | Tier migration focus |
| **Month 12+** | Optimization | 50% | Enterprise, add-ons |

---

### 8 Expansion Signals

**1. Usage Spikes**
- **Signal**: 50%+ increase in messages/automations month-over-month
- **Means**: Business is growing, current plan may limit growth
- **Action**: Reach out within 7 days of spike

**2. Plan Limit Proximity**
- **Signal**: At 70%+ of plan limits (messages, automations)
- **Means**: Will hit limits soon, frustration likely
- **Action**: Proactive outreach before limits hit

**3. Feature Requests**
- **Signal**: Asks for features in higher tiers
- **Examples**: "Can I add team members?", "Can I integrate with X?"
- **Means**: Expansion need identified
- **Action**: Explain tier benefits, offer upgrade path

**4. Multiple Logins**
- **Signal**: Different devices/IPs logging in
- **Means**: Team usage (possibly unauthorized)
- **Action**: Offer multi-user add-on

**5. High Engagement**
- **Signal**: Daily usage, high NPS (9-10), asks questions
- **Means**: Enthusiastic customer, sees value
- **Action**: Share advanced features, growth opportunities

**6. ROI Milestones**
- **Signal**: Achieves significant recovery milestone
- **Examples**: R$ 5.000+ recovered, 100+ no-shows prevented
- **Means**: Value realized, ready for more
- **Action**: Celebrate, share growth path

**7. Complaints About Limits**
- **Signal**: "Hitting message limit", "Need more automations"
- **Means**: Outgrew current plan
- **Action**: Immediate upsell outreach

**8. Business Changes**
- **Signal**: New location, new service line, team expansion
- **Means**: Business growth = product growth opportunity
- **Action**: Reach out within 30 days of change

---

### Outreach Cadence by Expansion Signal

**High-Intent Signals** (Plan limits, complaints, direct requests):
- **Timing**: Within 48 hours
- **Channel**: Phone call or WhatsApp
- **Tone**: Urgent, solution-focused
- **Goal**: Immediate upgrade

**Medium-Intent Signals** (Usage spikes, feature requests, multiple logins):
- **Timing**: Within 7 days
- **Channel**: Email + WhatsApp follow-up
- **Tone**: Helpful, opportunistic
- **Goal**: Schedule upgrade discussion

**Low-Intent Signals** (High engagement, ROI milestones):
- **Timing**: Next scheduled check-in (Day 90, 180, 360)
- **Channel**: In QBR or check-in call
- **Tone**: Celebratory, growth-focused
- **Goal**: Plant expansion seed

| Offer | Condition | Benefit |
|-------|-----------|---------|

| **Free Month** | Downgrade within 30 days | Credit for future use |

---

### Upgrade Pricing Psychology

**Anchor Pricing**:
- Show higher tier first ("Enterprise R$ 797+")
- Then show middle tier ("Scale R$ 397")
- Middle tier seems reasonable by comparison

**Decoy Pricing**:
- Starter: R$ 97 (500 messages) = R$ 0,194/message
- Growth: R$ 197 (2.000 messages) = R$ 0,099/message (52% savings)
- Scale: R$ 397 (10.000 messages) = R$ 0,040/message (79% savings)
- **Insight**: Higher tiers = better value per message

**Price Ending Strategy**:
- R$ 97 (not R$ 100) → Psychological pricing
- R$ 197 (ends in 7) → Feels like calculated discount
- R$ 397 (premium ending) → Perceived quality

## Part 9: Common Mistakes & Best Practices

### 7 Common Upsell Mistakes

**1. Upselling Too Early**
- **Problem**: Upselling before customer achieves value
- **Result**: High churn, frustration
- **Solution**: Wait until Month 4 OR R$ 1.000+ recovered

**2. One-Size-Fits-All**
- **Problem**: Same pitch for every customer
- **Result**: Low conversion, ignores customer context
- **Solution**: Personalize by industry, usage, goals

**3. Feature Dumping**
- **Problem**: Listing features instead of benefits
- **Result**: Overwhelming, no clear value
- **Solution**: Lead with pain, then solution

**4. Ignoring Price Sensitivity**
- **Problem**: Premium pricing without justification
- **Result**: Sticker shock, lost deals
- **Solution**: ROI calculator, payment terms, guarantees

**5. No Follow-Up**
- **Problem**: One outreach, then ghost
- **Result**: Low conversion (most need 5+ touches)
- **Solution**: 4-step sequence with persistence

**6. Confusing Upsell with Renewal**
- **Problem**: Upselling during renewal conversation
- **Result**: Negotiation on price, loses expansion leverage
- **Solution**: Separate conversations 60+ days apart

**7. Not Training CS Team**
- **Problem**: Success managers uncomfortable selling
- **Result**: Missed opportunities, low upsell rate
- **Solution**: Training, role-play, quotas, incentives

---

### 7 Upsell Best Practices

**1. Timing is Everything**
- Upsell when customer is winning (not struggling)
- Strike when expansion signal appears (within 7 days)
- Avoid month 1-3 (focus on success first)

**2. Lead with ROI**
- Don't sell features, sell results
- Calculate: "You invest R$ 100, gain R$ 2.000"
- Use customer's actual data whenever possible

**3. Make it Easy**
- One-click upgrade (in-app)
- Frictionless migration (we do the work)
- Free trials for add-ons
- Money-back guarantees

**4. Create Urgency (Legitimately)**
- Limited-time discounts (7-30 days)
- Price increases (annual notice)
- Feature availability (while spots)
- Business needs (seasonal growth)

**5. Social Proof**
- "Similar customers upgraded and saw [X] results"
- Case studies of tier migration success
- Testimonials from upgraded customers

**6. Tiered Incentives**
- Stack rewards (discount + setup + guarantee)
- Volume incentives (5+ users = 15% off)
- Long-term incentives (20% off annual)

**7. Measure Everything**
- Track upsell funnels by path
- A/B test everything (messaging, incentives, timing)
- Analyze by segment (clinics vs real estate)
- Optimize based on data, not intuition
