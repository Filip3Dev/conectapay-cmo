**Objective**:
- Operationalize health score methodology into an actionable Early Warning System
- Enable proactive churn prevention (7-30 days before churn)
- Standardize intervention responses across CS team
- Provide implementation roadmap from pre-launch to scale

**Deliverable Created**:
`customer-health-score-framework-and-early-warning-system.md` (comprehensive 1,100+ line framework)

**Key Components Created**:

1. **6-Dimension Health Score Framework**
   - Product Usage (30%): Messages, automation rules, payment volume, feature frequency
   - Customer Engagement (20%): Logins, response rate, feature exploration, content engagement
   - Payment Health (15%): Payment status, method, outstanding balance
   - Support Signals (15%): Ticket sentiment, volume, resolution time
   - Feature Adoption (10%): Core features, advanced features, integrations
   - Growth Signals (10%): Team size trend, volume trend, upgrade inquiries
   - Health categories: Green (80-100), Yellow (60-79), Orange (40-59), Red (0-39), Unknown (<7 days)

   - **Tier 1 (Yellow)**: Predictive alert, 7-day SLA, email notification
   - **Tier 2 (Orange)**: Warning alert, 48-hour SLA, email + Slack
   - **Tier 3 (Red)**: Critical alert, 4-hour SLA, email + Slack + SMS
   - **Tier 4 (Black)**: Emergency alert, 1-hour SLA, email + Slack + SMS + Phone
   - Escalation matrix with automatic tier progression
   - Alert suppression rules for onboarding/maintenance/known issues

3. **6 Intervention Playbook Library**
   - **P-1**: Predictive Outreach (Yellow) - 7 days, stabilize health
   - **P-2**: Warning Intervention (Orange) - 14 days, restore health to 70+
   - **P-3**: Critical Recovery (Red) - 30 days intensive, save from churn
   - **P-4**: Emergency Rescue (Black) - 7 days, last-ditch effort with CEO
   - **P-5**: Expansion Opportunity (Green) - 14 days, drive upgrades
   - **P-6**: New Customer Onboarding (Unknown) - 14 days, health >70 by day 14
   - Each playbook includes: call scripts, email templates, WhatsApp messages, success criteria

4. **4-Phase Implementation Roadmap**
   - Phase 1 (Week 1-2): Foundation - Data infrastructure, dashboard, playbooks
   - Phase 2 (Week 3-4): Pilot & Calibration - Test with 20-50 customers, refine model
   - Phase 3 (Week 5-8): Full Rollout - Expand to all customers, optimize
   - Phase 4 (Ongoing): Continuous Improvement - Quarterly reviews, A/B testing

5. **Pre-Launch Implementation Guide**
   - Phase 0 (Now): Prepare templates, choose CRM, set up Google Sheets MVP
   - Phase 1 (First 10 customers): Manual scoring, execute playbooks, learn
   - Phase 2 (First 50 customers): Automate scoring, basic dashboard, Tier 1 alerts
   - Phase 3 (First 100 customers): Full alert system, comprehensive dashboard
