## Executive Summary

This playbook is the **operational execution guide** for managing customer renewals and expansion revenue at ConectaPay. It bridges the gap between strategy (CLV optimization, upsell strategy) and day-to-day execution, providing CS and Sales teams with scripts, timelines, objection handling, and playbooks for every renewal and expansion scenario.

**Primary Objectives**:
- Achieve 95%+ renewal rate (target: 97%)
- Generate 20% of MRR from expansion revenue
- Maintain 110%+ Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Reduce churn to <3.5% monthly
- Ensure 0% "surprise churn" (all at-risk identified 60+ days out)

**Economic Impact**:
- At 500 customers: R$ 30.000/month expansion MRR = R$ 360.000/year
- 2% renewal rate improvement (95% → 97%): +10 customers retained = +R$ 23.640/year CLV saved
- NRR 110% = Grow revenue 10% even with 0 new customers

**Who Should Use This Playbook**:
- Customer Success Managers (daily renewal and expansion execution)
- Account Executives (enterprise renewals and large expansions)
- CS Leadership (forecasting, strategy, coaching)
- Sales Leadership (quota setting, pipeline management)

### 1.2 Renewal Risk Classification

Classify every renewal into **Green**, **Yellow**, or **Red** risk categories at Day -60:

**🟢 GREEN RENEWAL** (Low Risk, 70% of customers)
- **Criteria**:
  - Health score: 80+
  - Usage: Daily/weekly active
  - NPS: 9-10
  - Value achieved: R$ 2.000+ recovered
  - No complaints in last 90 days
  - Payment history: Clean (no failed payments)
- **Strategy**: Automated renewal with value reinforcement
- **Owner**: CSM (standard process)
- **Expected Renewal Rate**: 98%

**🟡 YELLOW RENEWAL** (Medium Risk, 20% of customers)
- **Criteria**:
  - Health score: 60-79
  - Usage: Weekly/monthly (inconsistent)
  - NPS: 7-8
  - Value achieved: R$ 500-2.000 recovered
  - 1-2 support tickets in last 90 days
  - Minor feature requests unaddressed
- **Strategy**: High-touch engagement, address concerns, demonstrate value
- **Owner**: Senior CSM
- **Expected Renewal Rate**: 85% (with intervention)

**🔴 RED RENEWAL** (High Risk, 10% of customers)
- **Criteria**:
  - Health score: <60
  - Usage: Rare or inactive
  - NPS: 0-6
  - Value achieved: <R$ 500 recovered
  - Multiple complaints or escalations
  - Payment issues or asked about cancellation
  - Competitor mentions
- **Strategy**: Executive engagement, retention offers, root cause analysis
- **Owner**: CS Manager + Founder (for strategic accounts)
- **Expected Renewal Rate**: 50% (with aggressive intervention)

### 1.5 Stakeholder Mapping for Renewals

Identify all stakeholders involved in renewal decisions:

**Primary Decision Maker** (The one who says yes/no):
- Roles: Owner, CEO, CFO, Practice Manager
- Signals: Payment method, account holder, contract signer
- Engagement: Direct outreach, phone call, in-person meeting

**Influencers** (Shape the decision):
- Roles: Office managers, receptionists, marketing team
- Signals: Daily users, advocates inside the organization
- Engagement: Ensure they're happy, arm them with ROI data

**Blockers** (Can say no but not yes):
- Roles: IT (technical concerns), Finance (budget concerns), Legal (contract review)
- Signals: Past objections, technical questions, payment delays
- Engagement: Preemptive objection handling, documentation

**Champions** (Will advocate for renewal):
- Roles: Power users, team members who benefit most
- Signals: High NPS (10), feature requests, referrals
- Engagement: Empower them, give them renewal talking points

## Part 2: Expansion Execution Playbook

### 2.1 Expansion Opportunity Identification

Identify expansion-ready customers using these **8 Expansion Signals**:

**Signal 1: Plan Limit Proximity** (Strongest Signal)
- **Trigger**: At 70%+ of plan limits (messages, automations)
- **Expansion Offer**: Tier upgrade
- **Timing**: Immediate outreach (within 48 hours)
- **Success Rate**: 35% conversion
- **Example**: "Você está em 1.400/2.000 mensagens. Vai bater no teto em 10 dias."

**Signal 2: Usage Growth Spike**
- **Trigger**: 50%+ increase in usage month-over-month
- **Expansion Offer**: Tier upgrade OR multi-user add-on
- **Timing**: Outreach within 7 days
- **Success Rate**: 28% conversion
- **Example**: "Seu uso cresceu 60% este mês. Parabéns pelo crescimento!"

**Signal 3: Team Usage Signals**
- **Trigger**: Multiple logins from different devices/IPs
- **Expansion Offer**: Multi-user add-on (R$ 49/user/month)
- **Timing**: Outreach within 7 days
- **Success Rate**: 32% conversion
- **Example**: "Notei que sua equipe também está usando. Que tal acesso oficial?"

**Signal 4: Feature Requests**
- **Trigger**: Asks for features in higher tiers
- **Expansion Offer**: Tier upgrade with requested feature
- **Timing**: Same-day response
- **Success Rate**: 40% conversion
- **Example**: "A feature que você quer está disponível no plano Growth."

**Signal 5: ROI Milestone Achieved**
- **Trigger**: R$ 5.000+ recovered OR 100+ no-shows prevented
- **Expansion Offer**: Tier upgrade (scaling opportunity)
- **Timing**: Next scheduled check-in
- **Success Rate**: 22% conversion
- **Example**: "Você já recuperou R$ 7.500. Vamos escalar esses resultados?"

**Signal 6: Business Expansion**
- **Trigger**: New location, new service line, team growth
- **Expansion Offer**: Tier upgrade + multi-user
- **Timing**: Within 30 days of news
- **Success Rate**: 38% conversion
- **Example**: "Nova unidade? O ConectaPay escala com você."

**Signal 7: Integration Requests**
- **Trigger**: Asks about API, CRM integration
- **Expansion Offer**: Integration Pack (R$ 147/month) OR Scale plan
- **Timing**: Within 48 hours
- **Success Rate**: 25% conversion
- **Example**: "Temos integração pronta com o seu CRM."

**Signal 8: Reporting/Analytics Needs**
- **Trigger**: Asks for reports, exports data frequently
- **Expansion Offer**: Advanced Analytics Dashboard (R$ 97/month)
- **Timing**: Within 7 days
- **Success Rate**: 30% conversion
- **Example**: "Precisa mostrar resultados aos sócios? Tenho o dashboard perfeito."

### 6.3 Red Renewal Escalation Matrix

**Level 1: CSM Outreach** (Day -60 to -45)
- **Owner**: Assigned CSM
- **Actions**: Phone call, value reinforcement, QBR scheduling
- **Success Rate**: 50% recovery

**Level 2: Senior CSM + Retention Offer** (Day -45 to -30)
- **Owner**: Senior CSM or CS Team Lead
- **Actions**: Executive outreach, custom retention offer, root cause analysis
- **Retention Offers**:
  - 20% discount for 3 months
  - 1 month free
  - Free setup/migration
  - Feature prioritization (roadmap)
- **Success Rate**: +20% recovery (70% cumulative)

**Level 3: CS Manager + Founder** (Day -30 to -15)
- **Owner**: CS Manager + Founder (for strategic accounts)
- **Actions**: Founder call, strategic partnership offer, custom solution
- **Founder Involvement Criteria**:
  - High-value account (R$ 10K+ CLV)
  - Strategic customer (reference case potential)
  - Level 1 and 2 failed
- **Success Rate**: +15% recovery (85% cumulative)

**Level 4: Let Churn** (Day -15 to 0)
- **Decision**: If Level 3 fails, let customer churn
- **Rationale**: Some customers are not a good fit. Forcing retention burns team time and damages reputation.
- **Actions**:
  - Graceful exit process
  - Offboarding data export
  - Win-back campaign (Day +30, +60, +90)
  - Request feedback (exit survey)

## Conclusion

**Key Takeaways**:

1. **90-Day Proactive Renewal Process**: Start at Day -60, not Day -30. Classify risk, engage early, prevent surprise churn.

2. **Renewal Risk Classification**: Green (70%, automated), Yellow (20%, high-touch), Red (10%, executive engagement). Allocate resources proportionally.

3. **Expansion = Revenue Growth**: 20% of MRR from expansion = R$ 30-60K/month at scale. 90% gross margin on expansion.

4. **Scripts Work**: Use the scripts in Part 3 and Part 4. Don't wing it. Consistency = higher conversion.

5. **Measure Everything**: Renewal rate, NRR, expansion revenue, pipeline, conversion rates. What gets measured gets managed.

6. **Team Accountability**: Clear roles (CSM, Senior CSM, CS Manager), quotas, and metrics. Everyone knows what success looks like.

7. **Continuous Optimization**: A/B test everything. Learn from every renewal and expansion conversation. Iterate constantly.

**Economic Impact**:
- 95% renewal rate = Retain more customers, reduce churn
- 20% expansion revenue = R$ 720K/year at 1.000 customers
- 110% NRR = Grow 10% even with 0 new customers

**Next Steps**:
1. Week 1-2: Build data foundation, train team
2. Week 3-4: Pilot with 20 customers
3. Week 5-8: Full launch to all customers
4. Week 9-12: Optimize, automate, scale

**Remember**: Renewals and expansions are not just about revenue. They're about customer success. If customers are successful, renewals and expansions follow naturally. Focus on value, ROI, and customer outcomes, and the numbers will take care of themselves.
