## Executive Summary

This index document provides a complete roadmap to ConectaPay's customer onboarding experience optimization framework. The framework is distributed across three comprehensive documents that together cover:

1. **Customer Journey Mapping** - From awareness to advocacy
2. **Onboarding Framework** - 14-day activation journey
3. **Optimization & TTFV Acceleration** - Advanced strategies for world-class performance

**Key Outcomes**:
- Activation Rate: 13.4% → 45% (+236% improvement)
- Time-to-First-Value: 48 hours → 12 hours (75% faster)
- Day 14 Retention: 80% → 95% (+19% improvement)
- Annual MRR Impact: R$ 1.14M (at 1,000 sign-ups/month)

## Quick Reference: Segment-Specific Onboarding

### Clínicas Médicas (40% of customer base)
**Primary Pain**: No-shows (R$ 200-500 per missed appointment)
**Key Strategy**: "No-Show Calculator" + "Clínica Success Pack" (4 templates)
**Target Metrics**: 92% activation, 18h TTFV, 95% Day 14 retention

### Imobiliárias (30% of customer base)
**Primary Pain**: Lead ghosting (80% of leads disappear)
**Key Strategy**: "Lead Recovery Calculator" + "Imobiliária Success Pack" (4 templates)
**Target Metrics**: 88% activation, 30h TTFV, 92% Day 14 retention

### Academias (20% of customer base)
**Primary Pain**: Member churn (10-15% monthly)
**Key Strategy**: "Churn Calculator" + "Academia Success Pack" (4 templates)
**Target Metrics**: 85% activation, 36h TTFV, 88% Day 14 retention

### Óticas (10% of customer base)
**Primary Pain**: Abandoned eyewear collections
**Key Strategy**: "Abandonamento Calculator" + "Ótica Success Pack" (3 templates)
**Target Metrics**: 82% activation, 12h TTFV, 90% Day 14 retention
