## Customer-Facing FAQ

### Getting Started

#### Q1: How do I get started with ConectaPay?

**A**: Getting started takes 5 minutes:

1. Create your account at [conectapay.com.br](https://conectapay.com.br)
2. Connect your WhatsApp Business (scan QR code)
3. Configure your Pix key
4. Create your first campaign
5. Start recovering lost sales!

Need help? Watch our [2-minute setup video](link) or [chat with us on WhatsApp](link).

---

#### Q2: Do I need WhatsApp Business?

**A**: Yes, ConectaPay works with WhatsApp Business API. If you don't have it:

1. Download WhatsApp Business from App Store/Play Store
2. Verify your phone number
3. Complete business profile setup
4. Connect to ConectaPay

It's free and takes 5 minutes to set up.

---

#### Q3: What if I don't have a Pix key?

**A**: You can create a Pix key in your bank app in 2 minutes:

1. Open your bank app
2. Go to "Área Pix" → "Chaves Pix"
3. Create a key (choose: phone, email, or random key)
4. Copy the key and paste in ConectaPay

Most banks support Pix. If yours doesn't, [contact us](link) for alternatives.

### Features & Usage

#### Q4: How does automated messaging work?

**A**: ConectaPay automatically sends WhatsApp messages when customers don't respond:

1. Customer starts a purchase/inquiry but doesn't complete
2. ConectaPay detects the "abandoned" interaction
3. After a set time (you choose), sends a personalized message
4. Customer responds and completes the purchase

You can customize:
- When to send (1 hour, 24 hours, etc.)
- What to say (personalized templates)
- Who to send to (filter by customer type)

---

#### Q5: Can I send messages at specific times?

**A**: Yes! ConectaPay respects business hours:

- Set your working hours (e.g., 9am-6pm)
- Messages only send during those times
- Weekend scheduling available
- Timezone support for all Brazilian regions

This ensures you don't wake customers at midnight!

---

#### Q6: How do I receive Pix payments?

**A**: ConectaPay integrates Pix directly into WhatsApp:

1. Customer agrees to purchase in chat
2. You (or system) sends Pix payment request
3. Customer pays with one click
4. Payment confirmed instantly
5. You receive notification

Funds go directly to your bank account (no intermediaries).

---

#### Q7: Can I see message history and responses?

**A**: Yes, ConectaPay tracks everything:

- **Messages sent**: View all automated messages
- **Responses received**: See customer replies
- **Conversion rate**: Track % of responses that lead to sales
- **Revenue recovered**: Total Pix payments received

Access this in your dashboard under "Campaigns → [Campaign Name] → Analytics".

### Technical Issues

#### Q11: WhatsApp messages not sending - what do I do?

**A**: Follow these steps:

1. **Check connection**: Go to Settings → WhatsApp → Status
2. **Reconnect if needed**: Click "Reconnect" and scan QR code again
3. **Check template**: Ensure message template is approved
4. **Check recipient**: Verify phone number format (+55 XX XXXXX-XXXX)

Still not working? [Contact support](link) - it's a P2 priority (4-hour response).

---

#### Q12: Pix payment not received - help!

**A**: This is critical (P1 priority). Check:

1. **Pix key correct**: Verify in Settings → Payments
2. **Bank status**: Check if your bank is processing Pix
3. **Transaction log**: View in ConectaPay under "Payments → History"

If funds don't appear in 1 hour, [contact support immediately](link) - we'll investigate.

---

#### Q13: Can't log in to my account

**A**: Try these solutions:

1. **Reset password**: Click "Esqueci a senha" on login page
2. **Clear browser cache**: Sometimes old data causes issues
3. **Try incognito mode**: Rules out browser extensions
4. **Check email**: Verify you're using the correct email

Still stuck? [Contact support](link) - P2 priority (4-hour response).

## Internal Support FAQ (For CS Team)

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Issue: Customer can't connect WhatsApp

**Troubleshooting Steps**:
1. Verify they have WhatsApp Business (not regular WhatsApp)
2. Check if WhatsApp is logged in on same phone
3. Ensure good internet connection
4. Try re-scanning QR code
5. Check if WhatsApp Business API is approved (can take 24-48 hours)

**If still failing**: Escalate to technical team (P2)

---

#### Issue: Pix payment not received

**Troubleshooting Steps**:
1. Verify Pix key is correct in ConectaPay
2. Check customer's bank status (is Pix down?)
3. Verify transaction in ConectaPay payment log
4. Check if customer's bank has delay
5. Confirm customer used correct Pix key

**If still failing**: Escalate to payment team (P1)

---

#### Issue: Customer wants to cancel

**Retention Script**:
1. **Understand reason**: "Can I ask why you're leaving?"
2. **Address concerns**: Offer solution if possible
3. **Offer alternatives**: Downgrade plan, pause account
4. **If firm**: Process cancellation immediately
5. **Request feedback**: Send exit survey

**Red flag**: If cancelling due to technical issue, DON'T cancel until fixed!

---

#### Issue: Customer angry about response time

**De-escalation Script**:
1. **Acknowledge**: "I apologize for the delay"
2. **Explain** (honesty): "We're experiencing higher volume"
3. **Prioritize**: "I'm escalating your ticket now"
4. **Compensate** (if severe): Offer free week/month
5. **Follow up**: Check in next day

**Prevention**: Monitor queue, auto-respond with expected wait time
