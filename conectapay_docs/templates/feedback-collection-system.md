### Survey Questions

#### Q1: On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend ConectaPay to other businesses?

**Options**: 0-10 scale (radio buttons)

**Follow-up** (if 9-10): "O que você mais gostou? (opcional)"
**Follow-up** (if 7-8): "O que precisamos melhorar para virar 10? (opcional)"
**Follow-up** (if 0-6): "O que podemos fazer melhor? (opcional)"

---

### NPS Calculation

**NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors**

- **Promoters (9-10)**: Love your product, will refer
- **Passives (7-8)**: Satisfied but not enthusiastic
- **Detractors (0-6)**: Unhappy, will churn or discourage others

**Example**:
- 50 responses
- 20 promoters (40%)
- 20 passives (40%)
- 10 detractors (20%)
- **NPS = 40 - 20 = +20**

**Benchmarks**:
- Excellent (SaaS): NPS 50+
- Good (SaaS): NPS 30-49
- Average (SaaS): NPS 10-29
- Poor (SaaS): NPS <10

---

### Action Thresholds

| Score | Category | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **9-10** | Promoter | Ask for referral, case study, testimonial |
| **7-8** | Passive | Ask what would make it a 10, improve experience |
| **0-6** | Detractor | Call within 48 hours, fix issues, prevent churn |

## Survey Infrastructure

### Tool Options

**Google Forms (Recommended - Free)**
- Unlimited surveys
- Up to 10,000 responses/month
- Basic analytics
- Easy to set up

**Typeform (Paid - Better UX)**
- Beautiful interface
- Advanced logic
- Better completion rates
- ~$30/month

**Survey Methods**

**Email Links**
- Create survey in Google Forms
- Get share link
- Add to email templates
- Track clicks in email software

**Embedded**
- Embed survey in customer portal
- Trigger in-app popups
- Higher completion rates

**WhatsApp Messages**
- Send survey link via WhatsApp
- Higher completion rates than email
- Good for quick surveys (NPS)

## Feedback Review Process

### Weekly (15 minutes)
- Review new survey responses
- Identify red flags (scores <3)
- Reach out to unhappy customers

### Monthly (1 hour)
- Aggregate survey data
- Identify trends/themes
- Present to product team
- Update roadmap based on feedback

### Quarterly (2 hours)
- Comprehensive feedback analysis
- NPS trend analysis
- Churn analysis
- Feature request prioritization
- Report to founder/stakeholders

## Feedback to Product Roadmap

### How We Use Feedback

1. **Collect feedback** (surveys, support, interviews)
2. **Categorize by theme** (UX, features, pricing, etc.)
3. **Count requests** (how many customers asked)
4. **Prioritize by impact** (high, medium, low)
5. **Add to roadmap** (next quarter, this quarter, now)
6. **Ship feature**
7. **Notify requesters** (feature is live!)
8. **Measure satisfaction** (did it solve the problem?)

### Example

**Feedback Received**: 10 customers requested "WhatsApp multi-agent support"

**Categorization**: Feature - Multi-agent

**Priority**: High (requested by power users, high LTV customers)

**Roadmap**: Add to Q2 2026

**Ship**: Release multi-agent feature in June 2026

**Notify**: Email all 10 requesters: "Feature you requested is live!"

**Measure**: Track adoption, NPS after release
