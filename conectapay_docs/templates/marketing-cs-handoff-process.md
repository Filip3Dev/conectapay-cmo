# Marketing-CS Handoff Process

**Purpose**: Seamless transition of leads from Marketing acquisition to Customer Success ownership
**Audience**: CMO (Marketing) → CS Hire (when hired)
**Last Updated**: April 27, 2026

---

## Handoff Triggers (When to Handoff)

### Trigger 1: Waitlist Signup
**Event**: Lead submits waitlist form (smoke test landing page)
**Marketing Action**:
- Add to Lead Tracking Dashboard (Master tab)
- Calculate ICP score (if industry/company size provided)
- Trigger welcome email automation
- Add to "Waitlist Nurture" sequence

**Handoff to CS**: Not yet - still in marketing nurture
**CS Action**: None (pre-launch)

### Trigger 2: ICP Interview Completed
**Event**: Interview finished, insights captured
**Marketing Action**:
- Update Lead Tracking Dashboard with interview notes
- Recalculate ICP score based on full qualification
- Move to "Qualified" or "Nurture" status
- Create handoff doc for qualified leads

**Handoff to CS**: For leads scoring 70+
**CS Action**:
- Review handoff doc
- Add to CRM as "Sales Qualified Lead"
- Schedule demo call (if smoke test live)
- Begin sales outreach sequence

### Trigger 3: Smoke Test Signup/Payment
**Event**: Lead creates account or makes first payment
**Marketing Action**:
- Mark as "Customer" in Lead Tracking Dashboard
- Create customer record in HubSpot
- Trigger onboarding sequence (Day 0 email)
- Create handoff doc with full lead history

**Handoff to CS**: Immediate
**CS Action**:
- Review handoff doc
- Accept customer ownership
- Monitor onboarding progress
- Reach out on Day 3 (check-in)
- Track health score starting Day 14

### Trigger 4: Customer Onboarding Complete
**Event**: Customer completes 14-day onboarding
**Marketing Action**: None (CS owns now)
**Handoff to CS**: CS has owned since signup
**CS Action**:
- Calculate first health score
- Schedule success call (Day 30)
- Ask for review/referral
- Add to "Active Customers" list

## Handoff Checklist

### Marketing Owner (CMO) - Before Handoff

**Lead Data**:
- [ ] Lead exists in Lead Tracking Dashboard
- [ ] ICP score calculated
- [ ] Source tracked (UTM parameters)
- [ ] Added to segment list (Clínica/Imobiliária/etc.)

**Qualification**:
- [ ] Industry verified
- [ ] Company size confirmed
- [ ] Pain severity assessed
- [ ] WhatsApp usage confirmed
- [ ] Budget authority identified

**Documentation**:
- [ ] Handoff doc created
- [ ] All interview notes included
- [ ] Objections documented with responses
- [ ] Next steps clearly defined

**CRM Update**:
- [ ] Lead/customer in HubSpot
- [ ] All properties populated
- [ ] Assigned to correct owner
- [ ] Lifecycle stage set

**Automation**:
- [ ] Welcome email scheduled
- [ ] Nurture sequence triggered (if applicable)
- [ ] Onboarding sequence triggered (if customer)

### CS Owner - After Handoff

**Within 24 Hours**:
- [ ] Handoff doc reviewed
- [ ] Lead/customer profile understood
- [ ] CRM record updated (if needed)
- [ ] Next action scheduled

**Within 1 Week**:
- [ ] Demo call completed (if qualified)
- [ ] Onboarding monitored (if customer)
- [ ] First check-in sent (Day 3 for customers)

**Within 30 Days**:
- [ ] Converted to customer (if qualified)
- [ ] Onboarding completed (if customer)
- [ ] Health score calculated (if customer)
- [ ] Success call scheduled (Day 30)

## Communication Between Marketing & CS

### Weekly Handoff Meeting (15 minutes)
**When**: Every Monday 10:00 AM
**Attendees**: CMO, CS Hire (when hired)

**Agenda**:
1. **Review Last Week's Handoffs** (5 min)
   - How many leads handed off?
   - How many converted to customers?
   - Any issues with handoff quality?

2. **Review This Week's Pipeline** (5 min)
   - How many leads ready to handoff?
   - Any high-priority leads?
   - Any red flags?

3. **Process Improvement** (5 min)
   - What's working well?
   - What needs to be fixed?
   - Action items for next week

### Ad-Hoc Communication
**Slack/WhatsApp Channel**: `#marketing-cs-handoff`

**When to message**:
- High-priority lead (ICP score 85+) ready for handoff
- Customer at risk (health score dropped)
- Urgent issue with onboarding
- Question about lead history

**Response time**:
- High-priority: Within 1 hour
- Normal priority: Within 4 hours
- Low priority: Within 24 hours

## Handoff Quality Metrics

### Track These Monthly

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Handoff Volume** | # of leads handed off per month | 50+ |
| **Handoff Accuracy** | % with complete handoff doc | 95%+ |
| **Time to First Contact** | CS contacts lead within X hours | <24 hours |
| **Conversion Rate** | % of handoffs that become customers | 20%+ |
| **Onboarding Success** | % completing 14-day onboarding | 90%+ |
| **Handoff Satisfaction** | CS satisfaction with handoff quality | 4.5/5 |

### Red Flags (Investigate If)
- Handoff accuracy <90% → Marketing process issue
- Time to first contact >48 hours → CS capacity issue
- Conversion rate <15% → Lead quality issue
- Onboarding success <80% → Product/CS issue

## Pre-Launch Mode (Current State)

**Since CS hire doesn't exist yet, CMO handles both Marketing AND CS**

### Current Handoff Process (Simplified)

1. **Marketing acquires lead** (Instagram DM, LinkedIn, waitlist)
2. **Marketing qualifies lead** (ICP score calculation)
3. **Marketing handoff to Marketing** (self-handoff)
4. **Marketing handles CS tasks** (onboarding, support, success)
5. **When CS hire joins**: Full handoff process kicks in

### Transition Plan (When CS Hire Joins)

**Week 1**: CS hire shadowing
- CMO owns all tasks
- CS hire observes handoffs
- CS hire learns CRM/dashboard

**Week 2**: Joint ownership
- CMO handles high-priority leads
- CS hire handles low/medium priority
- Joint handoff meetings

**Week 3**: CS hire takes over
- CS hire owns all new handoffs
- CMO available for escalation
- Weekly handoff meetings continue

**Week 4**: Full transition
- CS hire fully owns CS operations
- CMO focuses on marketing only
- Monthly handoff review (not weekly)
