## Executive Summary

Account-Based Marketing (ABM) para ConectaPay é uma estratégia focada em **contas de alto valor** (high-value accounts) dentro dos nossos 4 segmentos-alvo: clínicas médicas, imobiliárias, academias e óticas.

Ao contrário do marketing tradicional que lança uma rede ampla, ABM:
- **Foca em 50-100 contas premium** por segmento no Year 1
- **Personaliza mensagem e conteúdo** para cada conta específica
- **Alinha marketing e vendas** em objetivos compartilhados
- **Acelera pipeline** com decisores-chave

### Objetivos Year 1 (12 meses)

| Métrica | Baseline | Meta Year 1 |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Target Accounts (Total) | 0 | 200 (50/segmento) |
| Accounts Engaged | 0 | 120 (60% engagement) |
| Opportunities Created | 0 | 40 (33% conversion) |
| Deals Closed | 0 | 20 (50% win rate) |
| ARR from ABM | R$ 0 | R$ 240.000 (@ R$ 12k/ano médio) |

### 1.2 Account Sourcing Channels

#### Primary Sources (Priority Order)

1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** ⭐
   - Search: `Company size + Industry + Location`
   - Filter: 11-50, 51-200 employees
   - Keywords: "clínica médica", "imobiliária", "academia", "ótica"

2. **Google Maps + Google Business** ⭐
   - Search: "[Segmento] + [Cidade]"
   - Filter: Rating 4.0+, reviews 50+
   - Export: Name, address, phone, website

3. **Facebook/Instagram Business** ⭐
   - Search: Business pages in target cities
   - Filter: Followers 1.000+, active posts
   - Export: Page name, followers, contact

4. **Segment-Specific Directories**
   - **Clínicas**: Doctoralia, Doctoralia Brazil, Google "clínica + especialidade"
   - **Imobiliárias**: Portal do Imóvel, Zap Imóveis, Viva Real
   - **Academias**: Tecnofitness, Bodytech, Google "academia + cidade"
   - **Óticas**: Óticas populares, Google "ótica + shopping"

5. **Partner Referrals**
   - Ask existing partners (from CON-97)
   - Clinic SaaS, Real Estate CRM, POS providers
   - "Quem são seus top 20 clientes?"

### 1.3 Account Prioritization Matrix

Após identificar contas, classificar em 3 tiers:

#### Tier 1 (Strategic Accounts) - Top 10% por segmento
- **Critério**: Score 85+ na ICP Scorecard
- **Budget**: R$ 1.500-2.500/ano em marketing personalizado
- **Foco**: 5-10 contas/segmento (20-40 total)
- **Playbook**: 1-to-1 ABM (hyper-personalizado)

#### Tier 2 (Priority Accounts) - Próximos 30%
- **Critério**: Score 75-84 na ICP Scorecard
- **Budget**: R$ 500-1.000/ano em marketing segmentado
- **Foco**: 15-20 contas/segmento (60-80 total)
- **Playbook**: 1-to-Few ABM (pequenos grupos 5-10 contas)

#### Tier 3 (Target Accounts) - Últimos 60%
- **Critério**: Score 70-74 na ICP Scorecard
- **Budget**: R$ 100-300/ano em conteúdo segmentado
- **Foco**: 25-30 contas/segmento (100-120 total)

### 2.2 Research Methods

#### Passive Research (2-3 hours/account)
1. **Website deep dive** (30 min)
   - About us page
   - Blog/content strategy
   - Careers page (hiring = growth)
   - Case studies/testimonials

2. **Social media audit** (30 min)
   - Last 20 Instagram posts
   - Last 10 LinkedIn updates
   - Comments/engagement themes
   - Hashtag strategy

3. **Google search** (30 min)
   - News about company
   - Press releases
   - Interviews/founder quotes
   - Awards/recognition

4. **Review analysis** (30 min)
   - Google Maps reviews (last 50)
   - Facebook reviews
   - Reclame Aqui (check complaints)
   - Common praise/complaint themes

5. **LinkedIn company + stakeholder research** (30 min)
   - Employee count trend
   - Recent hires
   - Stakeholder backgrounds
   - Shared connections

#### Active Research (Outreach) (1-2 hours/account)
1. **Direct outreach via LinkedIn/Instagram**
   - Connect with 2-3 stakeholders
   - Ask 1-2 discovery questions
   - Request 15-min call

2. **Partner/peer referrals**
   - Ask mutual connections
   - Check with existing partners
   - Industry peer introductions

3. **Event participation**
   - Attend industry events
   - Network at conferences
   - Virtual roundtables

## 3. ABM Tiers & Playbooks

### 3.1 Tier 1: 1-to-1 ABM (Strategic Accounts)

#### Target: 20-40 accounts total
#### Investment: R$ 1.500-2.500/account/year
#### Timeline: 6-9 month sales cycle

#### Playbook Components

**1. Account-Specific Microsite** (R$ 500-800)
- Dedicated landing page: `conectapay.com.br/[company-name]`
- Personalized video from CEO/founder
- Custom ROI calculator with their data
- Case study from similar company
- Integration demo with their existing stack

**2. Direct Mail Package** (R$ 200-300)
- Personalized box with:
  - Custom printed report: "Oportunidade de Recuperação de Receita para [Company]"
  - Branded WhatsApp gadget (charging station, stand)
  - Handwritten note from founder
  - QR code to microsite
  - Offer: Free 90-day pilot

**3. Account-Based Advertising** (R$ 500-1.000)
- Meta Ads: Target employees by name + company domain
- LinkedIn Ads: Sponsored content to company followers
- Google Ads: Brand + competitor keywords
- Retargeting: Website visitors from company domain

**4. Multi-Channel Outreach Sequence** (8 weeks)

| Week | Channel | Message | Goal |
|------|---------|---------|------|
| 1 | LinkedIn InMail | Personalized connection + value prop | Accept connection |
| 2 | Physical mail | Direct mail package delivered | Create wow factor |
| 3 | Phone call | Follow-up on mail, 15-min request | Schedule discovery |
| 4 | WhatsApp (if number obtained) | Video message (Loom) from founder | Build trust |
| 5 | Email | Case study from similar company | Social proof |
| 6 | LinkedIn comment | Engage with their post authentically | Stay top of mind |
| 7 | Phone call | Second touch, urgency (pilot spots limited) | Schedule or commit to timeline |
| 8 | Email | Final value prop + alternative (demo, referral) | Close or nurture |

**5. Sales Enablement Materials**
- One-pager: "[Company] + ConectaPay: Partnership Proposal"
- Slide deck: 5 slides tailored to their KPIs
- ROI Calculator: Pre-filled with their metrics
- Integration diagram: Visual of their tech stack + ConectaPay

### 3.2 Tier 2: 1-to-Few ABM (Priority Accounts)

#### Target: 60-80 accounts total
#### Group size: 5-10 accounts per cohort
#### Investment: R$ 500-1.000/account/year
#### Timeline: 4-6 month sales cycle

#### Playbook Components

**1. Cohort-Specific Landing Page** (R$ 200-300 total)
- Shared page: `conectapay.com.br/clinicas-sp` or `/imobiliarias-rj`
- Video: Generic for cohort, but relevant
- ROI calculator with cohort averages
- 2-3 case studies from cohort

**2. Cohort-Based Content** (R$ 200-400)
- Webinar: "Recuperação de No-Show: Clínicas em SP"
- White paper: "Imobiliárias no RJ: Dobrando Conversão em WhatsApp"
- Case study compilation: 5 success stories from cohort

**3. Account-Based Advertising** (R$ 200-400)
- Meta Ads: Target employees from 5-10 companies in cohort
- LinkedIn Ads: Target by company name list
- Google Ads: Location + industry keywords

**4. Multi-Channel Outreach Sequence** (6 weeks)

| Week | Channel | Message | Goal |
|------|---------|---------|------|
| 1 | LinkedIn InMail | Cohort-focused connection (clínicas em SP) | Accept connection |
| 2 | Email | Cohort report (benchmark data) | Open + click |
| 3 | LinkedIn content | Tag in cohort-relevant post | Engagement |
| 4 | Phone call | Follow-up, invite to cohort webinar | RSVP to webinar |
| 5 | Webinar | Live event for cohort | Attend + demo request |
| 6 | Email | Post-webinar follow-up + pilot offer | Schedule demo |

## 4. Multi-Channel Orchestration

### 4.1 Channel Mix by Tier

| Channel | Tier 1 (1:1) | Tier 2 (1:Few) | Tier 3 (1:Many) |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| LinkedIn InMail | ✅ High | ✅ Medium | ⚠️ Low |
| LinkedIn Content | ✅ High | ✅ High | ✅ Medium |
| Email (1:1) | ✅ High | ✅ Medium | ✅ Automated |
| Direct Mail | ✅ High | ⚠️ N/A | ❌ No |
| Phone Call | ✅ High | ✅ Medium | ⚠️ Low |
| WhatsApp | ✅ High | ⚠️ If obtained | ❌ No |
| Meta Ads (Account-Based) | ✅ High | ✅ Medium | ❌ No |
| LinkedIn Ads | ✅ High | ✅ Medium | ⚠️ Low |
| Webinars | ⚠️ Invite-only | ✅ Cohort events | ✅ Segment events |
| Direct Mail | ✅ High | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| Events (In-Person) | ✅ Invite-only | ⚠️ Select events | ❌ No |

### 4.2 Channel Sequence Rules

#### Rule 1: Never Same Channel Twice in a Row
- Bad: Email → Email → Email
- Good: Email → LinkedIn → Phone → Email

#### Rule 2: Warm Before Hot
- Week 1-2: Passive channels (content, ads)
- Week 3-4: Active outreach (InMail, email)
- Week 5-6: Direct engagement (phone, WhatsApp)

#### Rule 3: Value Before Ask
- First 2 touches: Give value (report, case study, insight)
- Third touch: Soft ask (15-min call, webinar)
- Fourth touch: Direct ask (demo, pilot)

### 4.3 Cadence by Tier

#### Tier 1 Cadence (8 weeks, 8+ touches)
1. LinkedIn InMail (value)
2. Direct Mail (wow factor)
3. Phone Call (soft ask)
4. WhatsApp Video (trust building)
5. Email Case Study (social proof)
6. LinkedIn Engagement (stay top of mind)
7. Phone Call (urgency)
8. Email Final (close or nurture)

#### Tier 2 Cadence (6 weeks, 6 touches)
1. LinkedIn InMail (cohort invite)
2. Email Report (benchmark data)
3. LinkedIn Content Tag (engagement)
4. Phone Call (webinar invite)
5. Webinar (value + pitch)
6. Email Follow-up (demo offer)

#### Tier 3 Cadence (4 weeks, 4 touches)
1. LinkedIn Connection (segment relevance)
2. Email Report (waitlist)
3. LinkedIn Engagement (2-3 posts)
4. Email Case Study (demo offer)

### 5.3 Message Framework

#### Subject Line/Hook Formula
```
[Segment] + [Pain Point] + [Specific Metric] + [Social Proof]

Examples:
- "Clínicas em SP: Reduzindo no-show de 22% para 8%"
- "Imobiliárias RJ: Recuperando 47 leads abandonados/mês"
- "Academia FitPlus: Reduziu cancelamentos em 34%"
```

#### Opening Line Formula
```
[Observation] + [Empathy] + [Specific Insight] + [Bridge]

Examples:
- "Notei que sua clínica tem 4.8 estrelas no Google (impressionante!), mas estimamos que 22% dos agendamentos viram no-show. ConectaPay automatiza recuperação via WhatsApp."
- "Sua imobiliária postou sobre 50 novos imóveis esta semana (parabéns!). Quantos desses leads convertem em visitas? A média em RJ é 2.8%."
```

#### Value Proposition Formula
```
[Current State Pain] + [Desired State] + [Time to Value] + [Proof]

Examples:
- "Você está perdendo R$ 18.000/mês em no-shows. ConectaPay recupera 60% em 30 dias. Clínica DermatoVIP aumentou receita em R$ 12.000/mês."
- "Seus 47 leads abandonados representam R$ 47.000 em pipeline perdido. ConectaPay recupera 35% via WhatsApp. Imobiliária Castro dobrou conversões."
```

### 6.2 SLA (Service Level Agreement)

#### Marketing → Sales
**Within 24 hours**:
- New account identified → Add to Salesforce/CRM
- Account research complete → Notify sales owner
- Content requested → Deliver draft

**Within 1 week**:
- Account engaged → Book first discovery call
- Account in nurture → 3 meaningful touches

#### Sales → Marketing
**Within 24 hours**:
- Discovery call complete → Log feedback (pain points, budget, timeline)
- Content used → Report engagement (opened, clicked, shared)
- Account stalled → Flag for re-engagement

**Within 1 week**:
- New stakeholder identified → Update account map
- Competitive intelligence → Share with marketing
- Won/Lost → Complete debrief form

## 8. Tech Stack Recommendations

### 8.1 ABM Stack (Phase 1: MVP - R$ 0-500/mês)

#### Must-Have (Free/Low-Cost)
1. **CRM** (Free tier)
   - HubSpot CRM (free) or Pipedrive (from R$ 59/user/mês)
   - Track accounts, contacts, pipeline

2. **Spreadsheet/Database** (Free)
   - Google Sheets or Airtable (free tier)
   - Account research, scoring, planning

3. **LinkedIn** (R$ 179/mês for Sales Navigator Core)
   - Account search, stakeholder research
   - InMail credits, company insights

4. **Email** (R$ 50-100/mês)
   - Gmail/Google Workspace (existing)
   - Mail merge tool: Yet Another Mail Merge (free tier) or GMass

5. **WhatsApp** (R$ 0-50/mês)
   - WhatsApp Business (free) or Twilio API for automation (pay per message)

6. **Analytics** (Free)
   - Google Analytics 4 (free)
   - Meta Pixel (free)
   - LinkedIn Insight Tag (free)

#### Nice-to-Have (Scale Later)
1. **Account-Based Advertising**
   - Meta Ads: R$ 500-2.000/mês (ad spend)
   - LinkedIn Ads: R$ 1.000-3.000/mês (ad spend)
   - Google Ads: R$ 500-1.500/mês (ad spend)

2. **Direct Mail**
   - Sendoso (from R$ 50/package)
   - Local print + courier (R$ 30-50/package)

3. **Sales Engagement**
   - Outreach (from R$ 500/user/mês)
   - Salesloft (from R$ 450/user/mês)
   - Lemlist (from R$ 150/mês for email)
