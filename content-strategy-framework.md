# ConectaPay - Content Strategy Framework

## Purpose
Build initial content strategy to educate market, build trust, and generate demand before and during smoke test phase.

## Target Channels
- **LinkedIn**: B2B decision makers (clinic owners, real estate directors)
- **Instagram**: Visual storytelling, clinic/agency owners

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Pain & Problem (40% of content)
Educate about the hidden costs of abandoned sales and no-shows.

### Pillar 2: Solution & Education (30% of content)
Show how WhatsApp automation + Pix works to solve the problem.

### Pillar 3: Social Proof & ROI (20% of content)
Share case studies, calculations, and success stories.

### Pillar 4: Trust & Authority (10% of content)
Build credibility through data, research, and expertise.

## Content Production Guidelines

### Visual Style

**Brand Colors**:
- Primary: #00A884 (WhatsApp green)
- Secondary: #32BCAD (Pix teal)
- Accent: #FF4757 (Urgency red)

**Typography**:
- Headings: Bold, clear, readable
- Body: Sans-serif, 14-16px
- Data: Highlighted in color boxes

**Image Guidelines**:
- Use real clinic settings (authentic)
- Show diverse professionals (age, gender, ethnicity)
- Before/After comparisons (charts, screenshots)
- ROI calculations (clear, large numbers)

---

### Copywriting Principles

**DO**:
- Lead with pain and numbers
- Use specific R$ values
- Include testimonials and quotes
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use emojis moderately (1-2 per post)
- End with clear CTA

**DON'T**:
- Use technical jargon
- Make vague promises
- Write long paragraphs (max 3 lines)
- Feature-dump without benefit
- Forget the CTA
- Ignore mobile formatting

---

### Publishing Schedule

**LinkedIn**:
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-9am or 5-6pm
- Frequency: 3-4 posts/week
- Format mix: 50% carousel, 30% text, 20% video

**Instagram**:
- Best times: Mon-Wed, 12-1pm or 7-8pm
- Frequency: 4-5 posts/week + daily stories
- Format mix: 40% reels, 30% carousel, 30% stories

---

### Engagement Strategy

**Respond to every comment**:
- Within 1 hour for first 24 hours
- Use questions to continue conversation
- DM for qualified leads

**Engage with target audience**:
- Comment on prospect posts
- Share relevant industry content
- Tag strategic partners

**Track metrics**:
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Lead conversions (link clicks, DMs)
