# ConectaPay Pre-Launch CS Weekly Review Template

**Version**: 1.0
**Created**: April 27, 2026
**Owner**: CMO (acting CS lead until hiring)
**Frequency**: Weekly (every Monday)
**Duration**: 60 minutes

---

## Purpose

This weekly review adapts the standard CS operating rhythm for ConectaPay's pre-launch stage. Instead of reviewing actual customers, we focus on:
1. CS framework readiness
2. ICP discovery progress (CON-43)
3. Smoke test infrastructure readiness (CON-25)
4. Gap identification and action planning

---

## Transition to Post-Launch

Once we have 5+ paying customers, transition to the standard CS weekly review:

1. **Replace** "ICP Discovery Progress" with "Portfolio Health Review"
2. **Replace** "Smoke Test Readiness" with "At-Risk Customer Intervention"
3. **Add** "Expansion Opportunity Identification"
4. **Keep** "CS Framework Readiness" until all frameworks are implemented

---

## Success Criteria

**Pre-Launch Stage Success**:
- ✅ 15-20 customer interviews completed
- ✅ Smoke test infrastructure fully operational
- ✅ Clear ICP definition (1-2 segments validated)
- ✅ CS frameworks implemented and tested
- ✅ First paying customer onboarded successfully

**When to Transition**:
- Trigger: 5 paying customers active
- Handoff: Hire CS Manager or CSM
- Cadence: Continue weekly reviews with customer portfolio focus
