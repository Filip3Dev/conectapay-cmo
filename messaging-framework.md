## Channel-Specific Messaging

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
**Tone**: Urgent, problem-focused, social proof
**Format**: Short copy (max 125 characters primary text)
**CTA**: "Começar Agora", "14 Dias Grátis"
**Creative**: Before/after charts, testimonials

### Google Ads (Search)
**Tone**: Solution-focused, benefit-driven
**Format**: Headlines (max 30 chars), descriptions (max 90 chars)
**CTA**: "Configure Agora", "Teste Grátis"
**Keywords**: Problem-aware + solution-aware

### WhatsApp (Direct Outreach)
**Tone**: Personal, helpful, non-salesy
**Format**: Conversational, questions-driven
**CTA**: "Posso te enviar mais informações?", "Quer ver como funciona?"
**Approach**: Research interview first, pitch later

### Email (Follow-up)
**Tone**: Professional, informative, case study-driven
**Format**: Long-form, educational
**CTA**: "Ver case study", "Schedule demo"
**Content**: ROI calculator, testimonial videos

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Say)

### ❌ Feature-Focused (Wrong)
"Automated WhatsApp messaging system with API integration and real-time analytics dashboard"

### ✅ Benefit-Focused (Right)
"Recupere R$ 3.200/mês que sua clínica perde com no-shows"

### ❌ Vague Benefits (Wrong)
"Melhore sua eficiência e operationalize seus workflows"

### ✅ Specific Benefits (Right)
"Sua secretária ganha 3 horas por semana. Zero ligações manuais."

### ❌ Technical Jargon (Wrong)
"SaaS platform with omnichannel communication capabilities and payment gateway integration"

### ✅ Simple Language (Right)
"Envia lembretes no WhatsApp. Recebe via Pix. Pronto."

## Testing Hypotheses

### Hypothesis 1: Pain-Driven Wins
**Prediction**: Pain-focused messaging ("Perde R$ 2.500/mês") outperforms benefit-focused ("Ganhe 3 horas/semana")

**Test**: A/B test headlines
**Metric**: CTR and conversion rate

### Hypothesis 2: Social Proof Increases Conversion
**Prediction**: Landing pages with testimonials convert 40% better than pages without

**Test**: Split traffic between two landing page versions
**Metric**: Lead capture rate

### Hypothesis 3: ROI Calculator Increases Engagement
**Prediction**: Interactive ROI calculator increases time-on-page and conversion

**Test**: Heat map analysis + conversion tracking
**Metric**: Form completion rate

### Hypothesis 4: Pix Angle Resonates Strongest
**Prediction**: "Receba via Pix antes da consulta" resonates more than "Reduza no-shows"

**Test**: A/B test value props
**Metric**: Lead quality (willingness-to-pay)
