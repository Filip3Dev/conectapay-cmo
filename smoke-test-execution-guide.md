## Week 1: Launch & Initial Testing

### Day 1: Campaign Launch
**Budget**: R$ 200-300

**Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)**:
- Launch 4 ad sets (1 per headline angle)
- Budget: R$ 50-75 per ad set
- Target: Clínicas in São Paulo + Rio de Janeiro
- Age: 25-65
- Interests: Medicine, Small Business, Healthcare

**Google Ads**:
- Launch 3 ad groups (problem-aware, solution-aware, competitor)
- Budget: R$ 100 total
- Location: São Paulo + Rio de Janeiro
- Keywords: 15-20 total

### Day 2-3: Monitor & Optimize
**Check daily**:
- Impression share (target: 80%+)
- CTR (target: 2.5%+)
- CPC (target: R$ 1.50-2.50)
- Landing page visits
- Lead capture rate

**Kill losers immediately**:
- CTR < 1.5% after 1,000 impressions
- CPC > R$ 4.00 consistently
- Landing page bounce rate > 70%

### Day 4-5: Double Down on Winners
- Identify winning headline angle
- Identify winning creative format
- Pause bottom 50% of ad sets
- Reallocate budget to top performers

### Day 6-7: Weekly Review
**Metrics to analyze**:
- Total leads generated
- Cost per lead (CPL)
- Lead quality (WTP responses)
- Conversion rate by channel
- Top performing messages

**Action items**:
- Kill bottom 50% of ads
- Scale top 25% by 2-3x
- Test 1-2 new variations based on learnings

## Week 2: Optimization & Scaling

### Day 8-10: Scale Winners
**Budget**: R$ 600-800 (increase from Week 1)

**Actions**:
- Take winning ad set from Week 1
- Scale to 3x budget (R$ 150-225 per ad set)
- Expand to 3 more cities (Belo Horizonte, Brasília, Salvador)
- Test lookalike audience (based on website visitors)

### Day 11-12: Test New Variations
**A/B tests to run**:
- CTA: "Começar Agora" vs. "14 Dias Grátis"
- Social proof: With vs. without testimonial
- Pricing: Show price (R$ 297/mês) vs. hide price
- Landing page: Short vs. long form

### Day 13-14: Weekly Review
**Week 2 targets**:
- Total leads: 150-250
- CPL: R$ 10-20 (down from R$ 15-25)
- Conversion rate: 45%+

**Action items**:
- Kill all ads with CPL > R$ 25
- Scale winners with CPL < R$ 15
- Prepare for Week 3 expansion

---

## Week 3: Maximum Scale

### Day 15-17: National Expansion
**Budget**: R$ 1.000-1.200

**Actions**:
- Expand to all major Brazilian cities (top 20)
- Increase budget on winners to R$ 300-400 per ad set
- Test broad targeting (let algorithm find audience)
- Launch lookalike audiences (1%, 2%, 3%)

### Day 18-19: Creative Refresh
**Actions**:
- Create 2-3 new ad creatives (prevent fatigue)
- Test new video format (UGC style)
- Test carousel ads (multiple benefits)

### Day 20-21: Weekly Review
**Week 3 targets**:
- Total leads: 300-500
- CPL: R$ 8-15
- Conversion rate: 50%+

**Decision point**:
- If CPL < R$ 12 and conversion > 50%: Continue scaling
- If CPL > R$ 20 or conversion < 40%: Pivot messaging

---

## Week 4: Validation & Decision

### Day 22-24: Final Optimization
**Budget**: R$ 1.000-1.500

**Actions**:
- Kill all losing ads aggressively
- Scale only top 10% of performers
- Test retargeting (website visitors)
- Test email follow-up sequence

### Day 25-26: Lead Qualification
**Actions**:
- Contact all 500-800 leads generated
- Qualify based on: revenue, no-show volume, WTP
- Identify top 20% of leads (high value, ready to buy)
- Schedule demos with top 20%

### Day 27-28: Final Analysis
**Key metrics to calculate**:

**Lead Quality**:
- % of leads with R$ 2.000+ monthly loss
- % of leads with WTP > R$ 300/mês
- % of leads ready to buy now

**Unit Economics**:
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = Total ad spend / Paying customers
- LTV (Lifetime Value) = Revenue per customer × Months retained
- LTV:CAC ratio (target: 3x+)

**Channel Performance**:
- Meta Ads: CPL, conversion rate, lead quality
- Google Ads: CPL, conversion rate, lead quality
- Organic: % of total leads, quality comparison

**Decision Matrix**:
- ✅ **GO**: If CAC < R$ 100, LTV:CAC > 3x, 50+ qualified leads
- ⚠️ **PIVOT**: If CAC > R$ 150 OR LTV:CAC < 2x OR < 20 qualified leads
- ❌ **STOP**: If CAC > R$ 200 OR < 10 qualified leads

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low CTR (< 1.5%)
**Solution**:
- Pause ads immediately
- Test new headline angles
- Test new creative formats
- Refresh audience targeting

### Risk 2: High CPC (> R$ 4.00)
**Solution**:
- Improve ad relevance score
- Narrow audience targeting
- Test different ad placements
- Increase budget (algorithm needs data)

### Risk 3: Low Landing Page Conversion (< 30%)
**Solution**:
- A/B test value proposition
- Simplify form (reduce fields)
- Add social proof
- Improve mobile experience
- Test different CTAs

### Risk 4: Low Lead Quality (WTP < R$ 200)
**Solution**:
- Review targeting (wrong audience?)
- Review messaging (attracting wrong leads?)
- Add pricing to landing page (filter)
- Improve qualification questions

### Risk 5: Technical Issues (Pixel not firing, form broken)
**Solution**:
- Test everything before launch
- Set up alerts (GA, Meta, Google Ads)
- Have developer on standby
- Create fallback tracking (manual)

## Decision Points

### Day 7: Continue or Pivot?
**Continue if**:
- CTR > 2%
- CPL < R$ 25
- Conversion > 30%

**Pivot if**:
- CTR < 1.5% after 1,000 impressions
- CPL > R$ 40
- Conversion < 20%

### Day 14: Scale or Kill?
**Scale if**:
- CPL < R$ 20
- Conversion > 40%
- Lead quality > 50%

**Kill if**:
- CPL > R$ 30
- Conversion < 30%
- Lead quality < 30%

### Day 28: Go or No-Go?
**GO if**:
- 50+ qualified leads
- CAC < R$ 100
- LTV:CAC > 3x

**PIVOT if**:
- 20-50 qualified leads
- CAC R$ 100-150
- LTV:CAC 2-3x

**STOP if**:
- < 20 qualified leads
- CAC > R$ 150
- LTV:CAC < 2x

## Next Steps After Smoke Test

### If GO (Decision: Build Product)
1. Hire/onboard 2-3 customers (beta)
2. Build MVP based on interview insights
3. Launch in 4-6 weeks
4. Scale customer acquisition

### If PIVOT (Decision: Refine Strategy)
1. Analyze why leads didn't convert
2. Test new messaging/positioning
3. Test different niches (real estate?)
4. Run second smoke test in 2 weeks

### If STOP (Decision: Not Validated)
1. Review all interview data
2. Identify patterns (what did we miss?)
3. Test new problem hypothesis
4. Or pivot to different segment entirely
