## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive B2B demand generation strategy for ConectaPay, designed to drive qualified pipeline across 4 priority segments (Clínicas, Imobiliárias, Academias, Óticas). The strategy combines paid media, account-based marketing, content marketing, and nurture programs to create a predictable revenue engine.

**Key Objectives**:
- Generate 500 qualified leads per month by Month 6
- Achieve 15% lead-to-customer conversion rate
- Maintain CAC < R$ 400 across all channels
- Build sustainable pipeline with 3x coverage

**Target Segments** (from [CON-124](/PAP/issues/CON-124)):
1. 🥇 Clínicas Médicas (Tier 1: 5-20 employees, R$ 50K-200K/mo revenue)
2. 🥈 Imobiliárias (10-50 agents, R$ 100K-500K/mo commission)
3. 🥉 Óticas (3-8 employees, R$ 30K-80K/mo revenue)
4. 4️⃣ Academias (5-15 staff, 500-2,000 members)

## Part 1: Demand Generation Strategy Overview

### 1.1 Core Philosophy

**Pain-Driven, Multi-Touch, Data-Backed**

1. **Pain-Driven**: Lead with quantified revenue loss, not feature lists
2. **Multi-Touch**: 7-12 touchpoints before conversion (across channels)
3. **Data-Backed**: ROI calculator, case studies, social proof at every stage

## Part 2: Top-of-Funnel (TOFU) Campaigns

### 2.1 Campaign 1: "Calculate Your Loss" (Instagram + Facebook)

**Objective**: Drive problem awareness at scale

**Target**: Tier 1 Clínicas (5-20 employees, state capitals)

**Creative Strategy**:
- **Format**: Carousel ads (4 cards)
- **Hook Card**: "Sua clínica perde R$ 5.000-15.000 por mês com no-shows"
- **Proof Card**: "Calculadora: veja seu prejuízo em 30 segundos"
- **Solution Card**: "Clínicas recuperam R$ 3.200 no primeiro mês"
- **CTA Card**: "Calcular meu prejuízo agora"

**Audience**:
- **Demographics**: 25-55 years old, business owners/managers
- **Interests**: Clinic management, medical practice, healthcare business
- **Behaviors**: Facebook Page admins, business tools users
- **Location**: São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Belo Horizonte, Curitiba, Salvador
- **Estimated Reach**: 150,000

**Budget**: R$ 150/day (R$ 4,500/month)

**Success Metrics**:
- CTR: >2.5%
- Landing page conversion: >15%
- Cost per lead: <R$ 150
- Target: 30 leads/day

---

### 2.2 Campaign 2: "No-Show Solution" (Google Search)

**Objective**: Capture high-intent problem-aware traffic

**Target**: Actively searching for no-show solutions

**Keyword Strategy**:

**Problem-Aware Keywords (High Intent)**:
- "reduzir no-show clínica"
- "sistema de confirmação consulta"
- "lembrrete whatsapp automático"
- "recuperar faltas clínica"
- "diminuir ausências pacientes"

**Solution-Aware Keywords (Medium Intent)**:
- "software agenda clínica whatsapp"
- "automação para clínicas"
- "sistema de agendamento automático"
- "ferramenta confirmação consulta"

**Budget**: R$ 100/day (R$ 3,000/month)

**Ad Copy Example**:
```
Headline: Reduza No-Shows em 70% | ConectaPay
Description: Automatize lembretes via WhatsApp. Sua clínica recupera
R$ 3.000-15.000/mês. Configure em 15 minutos. 14 dias grátis.
CTA: Testar Grátis
```

**Success Metrics**:
- CTR: >4%
- Quality Score: >7
- Cost per click: <R$ 8
- Cost per lead: <R$ 200
- Target: 15 leads/day

### 2.3 Campaign 3: "LinkedIn Direct" (ABM Lite)

**Objective**: Reach high-value clinic owners and decision makers

**Target**: Owners and directors of Tier 1 clinics

**Approach**:
1. **Connection Requests**: 20/day (600/month)
   - Personalized based on clinic profile
   - Reference mutual connections or posts

2. **InMail Sequence**: 50/week to non-connections
   - Value-first approach (case study, not pitch)
   - Soft CTA: "Open to seeing how this works?"

3. **Content Strategy**: 3 posts/week
   - Pain-focused: "The R$ 5.000/month leak in your clinic"
   - Solution-focused: "How clinics cut no-shows by 70%"
   - Social proof: "Clínica X recovered R$ 8.500 in month 1"

**Budget**: R$ 50/day (R$ 1,500/month) + LinkedIn Sales Navigator

**Success Metrics**:
- Connection acceptance: >25%
- InMail response: >15%
- Content engagement: >5%
- Target: 40 leads/month

## Part 5: Account-Based Marketing (ABM)

### 5.1 ABM Strategy: "Top 100 Clinics"

**Objective**: Land and expand highest-value accounts

**Target**: Top 100 clinics by revenue in each metro area

**Selection Criteria**:
- 20+ employees
- R$ 200K+ monthly revenue
- 300+ appointments/week
- Tech-savvy (already use SaaS)
- Growing (hiring, expanding)

**Approach**:

**1. Identify** (Week 1)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator search
- Google Maps scraping
- Doctoralia premium list
- Instagram business accounts

**2. Research** (Week 1-2)
- Website analysis
- Social media activity
- Recent posts (growth, hiring)
- Current tech stack
- Decision maker identification

**3. Multi-Channel Outreach** (Week 2-8)

**Channel 1: LinkedIn**
- Connection request + personalized note
- Content engagement (like, comment)
- InMail with value (case study)
- 3 touchpoints over 2 weeks

**Channel 2: Email**
- Personalized to decision maker
- Subject line references their clinic
- Value-first (no pitch in email 1)
- 4 touchpoints over 3 weeks

**Channel 3: WhatsApp**
- Direct number from website
- Personalized based on research
- Social proof from similar clinics
- 3 touchpoints over 2 weeks

**Channel 4: Direct Mail**
- Physical package with:
  - Printed case study
  - Handwritten note
  - ROI calculation for their clinic
  - QR code to demo booking

**4. Nurture** (Week 8+)
- Monthly newsletter with industry insights
- Quarterly check-in calls
- Exclusive webinars for ABM accounts
- Priority support if they convert

**Success Metrics**:
- Accounts engaged: 60% (60/100)
- Demos booked: 30% (18/60)
- Deals closed: 50% of demos (9 accounts)
- Revenue per account: R$ 6,000-12,000/year
- ROI: 10x on ABM investment

### 7.3 Attribution Model

Use multi-touch attribution to understand the customer journey.

**Model**: Time-decay attribution (recent touchpoints get more credit)

**Example Journey**:
```
Day 0:  Instagram Ad (10% credit)     → First touch
Day 3:  Email #1 (10% credit)         → Nurture
Day 7:  WhatsApp Message (20% credit) → Engagement
Day 10: Email #3 (20% credit)         → Education
Day 14: Google Search (30% credit)    → Intent
Day 15: Demo booking (10% credit)     → Conversion
```

**Why This Model**:
- Rewards all touchpoints (not just last-click)
- Values recent interactions more (they drove decision)
- Reflects real buying journey (multi-touch, multi-week)

## Part 9: Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: High CAC

**Mitigation**:
- Focus on high-intent channels (Google Search)
- Improve landing page conversion (A/B test)
- Increase email-to-demo conversion (better nurture)
- Leverage organic channels (content, SEO)

**Trigger**: CAC > R$ 500 for 2 weeks

**Action**: Pause underperforming channels, double down on winners

---

### Risk 2: Low Lead Quality

**Mitigation**:
- Tighten audience targeting
- Improve lead scoring model
- Add qualification questions to forms
- Use disqualification criteria

**Trigger**: Qualified lead rate < 20%

**Action**: Review targeting, adjust messaging, add friction

---

### Risk 3: Low Conversion to Paid

**Mitigation**:
- Improve onboarding experience
- Add success manager support
- Send proactive check-in messages
- Create milestone celebrations

**Trigger**: Trial-to-paid < 30%

**Action**: Interview churned users, fix onboarding gaps

---

### Risk 4: Ad Fatigue

**Mitigation**:
- Refresh creatives every 4 weeks
- Rotate ad formats (carousel, video, stories)
- Test new audiences and lookalikes
- Diversify channels

**Trigger**: CTR decline > 30% from peak

**Action**: Introduce new creative, pause old ads
