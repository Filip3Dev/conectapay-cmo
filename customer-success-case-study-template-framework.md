# ConectaPay - Customer Success Case Study Template Framework

## Purpose
This framework provides templates, guidelines, and processes for creating compelling customer success stories that build trust, validate our value proposition, and drive conversions.

**Use this document when:**
- Creating case studies for website, social media, or sales materials
- Interviewing customers about their results
- Writing testimonials or success stories
- Training team members on how to capture customer success

---

## Case Study Philosophy

### What Makes a Great ConectaPay Case Study?

**✅ Great Case Study:**
- **Leads with pain**: "Dra. Ana perdia R$ 2.500/mês com no-shows"
- **Specific numbers**: "Recuperou R$ 3.200 no primeiro mês"
- **Before/After**: Clear transformation with metrics
- **ROI focused**: Investment vs. return clearly shown
- **Authentic voice**: Customer quotes in their own words
- **Brazilian context**: WhatsApp, Pix, local business reality

**❌ Weak Case Study:**
- Vague praise: "Ótimo produto, recomendo!"
- Feature focus without results
- No specific metrics or ROI
- Generic testimonial without context
- Corporate language, no personality

## Case Study Structure Template

### The ConectaPay Case Study Formula

Every case study follows this proven structure:

```
1. HOOK (First line)
   Customer name + location + specific pain

2. BEFORE (The Problem)
   What was happening
   Specific metrics of loss
   Why current solutions failed

3. TRANSITION (The Decision)
   Why they chose ConectaPay
   Implementation process
   Time to see results

4. AFTER (The Results)
   Specific improvements
   Quantified ROI
   Unexpected benefits

5. QUOTE (The Voice)
   Direct customer quote
   In their own words

6. CTA (The Action)
   What the reader should do next
```

## Customer Interview Guide

### How to Extract a Great Case Study

Use this interview guide to capture the information needed for compelling case studies.

**Preparation:**
- Review customer's data beforehand (revenue, usage metrics)
- Send questions in advance so they can prepare
- Schedule 30-45 minutes for the interview
- Record with permission (or take detailed notes)

---

### Interview Questions (in Order)

#### Section 1: Context & The Problem (5-7 minutes)

**Warm-up:**
1. "Conte um pouco sobre sua [clínica/imobiliária/empresa]. Há quanto tempo está no mercado?"
2. "Quantos [clientes/pacientes/atendimentos] vocês têm por [semana/mês]?"

**The Problem Discovery:**
3. "Como vocês gerenciavam [lembretes/pagamentos/follow-ups] antes da ConectaPay?"
4. "Qual era o maior desafio nesse processo?"
5. "Você chegou a calcular quanto estava perdendo com [no-shows/vendas abandonadas]?"
6. "Me conta sobre uma situação específica que foi frustrante."

**Quantifying the Pain:**
7. "Se você tivesse que estimar, quanto isso custava por mês?"
8. "Além do dinheiro, qual era o impacto na [equipe/operação/saúde mental]?"

#### Section 2: The Solution Decision (3-5 minutes)

**Finding ConectaPay:**
9. "Como você conheceu a ConectaPay?"
10. "O que te fez decidir tentar?"

**Implementation:**
11. "Como foi o processo de configuração?"
12. "Quanto tempo levou para ver os primeiros resultados?"
13. "Teve algum desafio técnico? Como a gente ajudou?"

#### Section 3: The Results (5-7 minutes)

**Specific Metrics:**
14. "Qual foi a primeira melhoria que você notou?"
15. "Me dá números específicos: antes você tinha [X], agora tem [Y]?"
16. "Quantos [no-shows/vendas perdidas] você evitou no primeiro mês?"
17. "Qual foi o impacto financeiro? Quanto recuperou?"

**Unexpected Benefits:**
18. "Teve algum benefício que você não esperava?"
19. "Sua equipe notou diferença? O que eles comentam?"

#### Section 4: The Quote & Recommendation (2-3 minutes)

**Customer Voice:**
20. "Se você tivesse que resumir sua experiência em uma frase, o que diria?"
21. "O que você diria para alguém que está em dúvida sobre usar ConectaPay?"
22. "Tem algo mais que gostaria de compartilhar?"

---

### Probing Questions to Get Specific Numbers

If the customer gives vague answers, use these probes:

**Instead of:** "Melhorou bastante"
**Ask:** "Melhorou 10%? 50%? 100%?"

**Instead of:** "Recuperei muito dinheiro"
**Ask:** "Consegue estimar em reais? R$ 1.000? R$ 3.000?"

**Instead of:** "Minha equipe gostou"
**Ask:** "Quantas horas por semana vocês economizam?"

**Instead of:** "Foi rápido"
**Ask:** "Quanto tempo exatamente? 15 minutos? 1 hora?"

## Metrics to Capture

### Primary Metrics (Must-Have)

Every case study MUST include these:

1. **Revenue Recovery**: R$ amount per month
2. **No-Show Reduction**: Percentage decrease
3. **ROI**: Multiple of investment (e.g., 6x)
4. **Payback Period**: Days to recover investment
5. **Time to Results**: Days/weeks to see first results

### Secondary Metrics (Nice-to-Have)

Include 2-3 of these:

1. **Pix Adoption Rate**: Percentage using advance payment
2. **Staff Time Saved**: Hours per week
3. **Confirmation Rate**: Percentage improvement
4. **Cash Flow Improvement**: R$ amount from advance payments
5. **Customer Satisfaction**: Patient/client feedback

### Context Metrics (Essential for Credibility)

Always include:

1. **Business Type**: Clinic specialty, real estate focus, etc.
2. **Business Size**: Number of doctors/agents/locations
3. **Location**: City and state
4. **Implementation Date**: When they started
5. **Setup Time**: How long to get running

## Case Study Quality Checklist

Before publishing any case study, ensure:

### Content Quality
- [ ] Includes specific R$ amounts
- [ ] Has clear before/after comparison
- [ ] ROI is calculated and shown
- [ ] Customer quote is authentic and powerful
- [ ] Problem is clearly defined
- [ ] Solution is simply explained
- [ ] Results are quantified with metrics

### Brand Alignment
- [ ] Follows brand voice (direct, pain-driven)
- [ ] Uses Brazilian context (WhatsApp, Pix)
- [ ] Tone is trustworthy and relatable
- [ ] No jargon or corporate language
- [ ] Mobile-optimized formatting

### Credibility
- [ ] Customer has approved final version
- [ ] Metrics are accurate and verifiable
- [ ] Context is complete (industry, size, location)
- [ ] Implementation time is specified
- [ ] Unexpected challenges mentioned (if any)

### Production
- [ ] Visuals are professional
- [ ] Charts are clear and accurate
- [ ] Customer photo/logo included
- [ ] Multiple formats created (web, PDF, social)
- [ ] SEO-optimized (if on website)

## Common Case Study Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake 1: Vague Results
**Bad:** "Melhorou muito a operação"
**Good:** "Reduziu no-shows de 12 para 3 por semana (75%)"

### ❌ Mistake 2: No Context
**Bad:** "Recuperou R$ 3.200"
**Good:** "Clínica de 3 dermatologistas em SP recuperou R$ 3.200/mês"

### ❌ Mistake 3: Corporate Speak
**Bad:** "Otimizamos a eficiência operacional"
**Good:** "A secretária ganhou 3 horas por dia"

### ❌ Mistake 4: Missing ROI
**Bad:** "Vale muito a pena"
**Good:** "ROI de 10x. Pagou-se em 3 dias."

### ❌ Mistake 5: Generic Quotes
**Bad:** "Produto excelente!"
**Good:** "Recuperei o investimento em 3 dias. Depois disso é lucro."

## Questions or Need Help?
