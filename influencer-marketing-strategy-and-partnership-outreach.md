# ConectaPay Influencer Marketing Strategy and Partnership Outreach

## Executive Summary

**Objective**: Scale ConectaPay awareness and lead generation through strategic partnerships with Brazilian influencers, content creators, and thought leaders in our target segments (Clínicas, Imobiliárias, Academias, Óticas).

**Target**: 15-20% of total growth from influencer channels by Year 2

**Core Hypothesis**: Micro and mid-tier influencers (10K-100K followers) in niche healthcare, real estate, and business communities will drive higher-quality leads at lower CAC than macro-influencers, due to audience trust and segment specificity.

**Economics**:
- Influencer fee: R$ 500-5.000 per post (varies by tier)
- Affiliate commission: 20-25% of first-year revenue (FYR)
- Expected CAC: R$ 80-150 (including fee + commission)
- Target ROAS: 3-5x on influencer investment

---

## Part 1: Influencer Tier System

### Tier 1: Nano Influencers (1K-10K followers)

**Profile**:
- High engagement rate (8-15%)
- Niche authority (specific segment: dermatologists, clinic owners, real estate agents)
- Strong community trust
- Limited professional content production experience

**Compensation Model**:
- **Base fee**: R$ 200-500 per post
- **Affiliate commission**: 25% of FYR
- **Free product**: Free ConectaPay access (Tier 1 plan)

**Target Segments**:
- Clínica owners with active Instagram presence
- Corretores de imóveis with YouTube channels
- Personal trainers/academia owners on Instagram/TikTok
- Ótica owners sharing business tips

**Campaign Types**:
- Product review posts
- "Dia na vida" content showing ConectaPay usage
- Before/after case studies
- Instagram Reels tutorials

**Engagement Rate**: 8-15%
**Expected Reach**: 5K-50K impressions per post
**Expected Conversions**: 3-8 leads per post
**CAC**: R$ 60-100

---

### Tier 2: Micro Influencers (10K-50K followers)

**Profile**:
- Strong engagement rate (5-10%)
- Recognized in niche (healthcare entrepreneurs, real estate educators)

**Profile**:

- Major Brazilian business influencers

**Campaign Types**:
- High-production review videos
- Multi-platform launch campaigns

**Engagement Rate**: 2-5%
**Expected Reach**: 750K-2.5M impressions per post
**Expected Conversions**: 80-200 leads per post
**CAC**: R$ 150-250

**⚠️ STRATEGIC NOTE**: Use sparingly. Only for major product launches or brand awareness campaigns. Focus 80% of budget on Tiers 1-3.

## Part 3: Influencer Qualification Scorecard

### Influencer Fit Score (0-100 points)

**Use this scorecard to evaluate potential influencers before outreach**.

| Criteria | Points | Scoring Guide |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Segment Alignment** | 0-20 | 18-20: Perfect fit (exact target segment) <br> 15-17: Strong fit (closely related segment) <br> 10-14: Moderate fit (adjacent segment) <br> 0-9: Weak fit (general audience) |
| **Audience Quality** | 0-15 | 13-15: High engagement (8%+), authentic comments, no fake followers <br> 10-12: Good engagement (5-7%), mostly real audience <br> 7-9: Average engagement (3-4%), some questionable followers <br> 0-6: Low engagement (<3%), bought followers likely |
| **Content Quality** | 0-15 | 13-15: Professional, consistent, on-brand potential <br> 10-12: Good quality, some inconsistency <br> 7-9: Decent quality, amateur production <br> 0-6: Poor quality, not brand-safe |
| **Brand Safety** | 0-15 | 13-15: Clean history, professional, no controversies <br> 10-12: Mostly safe, minor issues <br> 7-9: Some risks, needs review <br> 0-6: High risk, avoid |
| **Partnership Experience** | 0-10 | 9-10: Extensive brand partnerships, professional <br> 7-8: Some partnerships, capable <br> 5-6: Few partnerships, needs guidance <br> 0-4: No experience, high effort |
| **Geographic Fit** | 0-10 | 9-10: Brazil-based, audience in target cities <br> 7-8: Brazil-based, national audience <br> 5-6: Partial Brazil presence <br> 0-4: Limited Brazil relevance |
| **Cost Efficiency** | 0-10 | 9-10: Excellent CPM (<R$ 50), high ROI potential <br> 7-8: Good CPM (R$ 50-100) <br> 5-6: Fair CPM (R$ 100-150) <br> 0-4: Expensive (CPM >R$ 150) |
| **Response Rate** | 0-5 | 5: Responded within 24h <br> 4: Responded within 48h <br> 3: Responded within 72h <br> 0-2: Slow response (>72h) |

**QUALIFIED**: 60+ points → Proceed to negotiation
**MAYBE**: 50-59 points → Consider if strategic value
**NOT QUALIFIED**: <50 points → Decline or nurture for later

### Phase 3: Negotiation & Agreement (Week 5-6)

**Negotiation Framework**:

**For Nano Influencers (Tier 1)**:
- Offer: Free access + 25% commission (no base fee)
- Content expectation: 1-2 posts per month
- Exclusivity: None (can work with competitors)
- Contract: Simple affiliate agreement

**For Micro Influencers (Tier 2)**:
- Offer: R$ 1.000-2.000 fee + 22% commission
- Content expectation: 2-3 posts per month
- Exclusivity: Category-exclusive (no other WhatsApp automation tools)
- Contract: 3-month renewable agreement

**For Mid-Tier Influencers (Tier 3)**:
- Offer: R$ 3.000-6.000 fee + 20% commission
- Content expectation: 3-5 posts across platforms
- Exclusivity: Category-exclusive (no automation tools)
- Contract: 6-month renewable agreement + performance bonus

**For Macro Influencers (Tier 4)**:
- Offer: R$ 10.000-20.000 fee + 18% commission
- Content expectation: Multi-platform campaign
- Exclusivity: Negotiable (often requested)
- Contract: 12-month agreement + revenue share

## Part 5: Campaign Types & Content Briefs

### Campaign Type 1: Product Review (Single Post)

**Best For**: Nano and Micro influencers (Tiers 1-2)

**Content Structure**:
1. **Hook (first 3 seconds)**: Pain point + specific loss
2. **Problem**: Describe the challenge (no-shows, abandoned leads)
3. **Solution**: Introduce ConectaPay
4. **Demo**: Show how it works (screen recording, WhatsApp walkthrough)
5. **Results**: Share specific numbers (if available)
6. **CTA**: Link with tracking code

**Example Brief (Clínica Influencer)**:
```
TÍTULO: Como reduzi no-shows em 70% (e recuperei R$ 3.000/mês)

ROTEIRO SUGERIDO:
- 0-5s: "Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500+ todo mês com no-shows?"
- 5-20s: "Eu também perdia. 2-3 pacientes por dia. R$ 600 por semana."
- 20-40s: "ConectaPay automatiza lembretes via WhatsApp. Configurei em 15 minutos."
- 40-55s: "Resultado: No-shows caíram de 12 para 3 por semana. Recupero R$ 3.000/mês."
- 55-60s: "Teste grátis: [LINK] (se ganha R$ 3.000, vale a pena, né?)"

FORMATO: Instagram Reel (vertical, 60 segundos)
ESTILO: Conversational, camera talking head + screen recording
MÚSICA: Upbeat but not distracting
HASHTAGS: #clinica #medicina #noshow #automação #whatsapp
DISCLOSURE: #ad ou #parceria no final
```

**Expected Performance**:
- Views: 5K-50K (varies by follower count)
- Engagement: 5-10%
- Clicks: 2-5% of views
- Leads: 3-10 from single post

## Part 6: Influencer Campaign Tracking & Analytics

### Tracking Setup

**1. Attribution Model**

**Primary Model**: Last-click attribution
- Influencer gets credit for lead if their link was the last click before signup

**Secondary Model**: Multi-touch attribution
- Influencer gets partial credit if touched at any point in journey
- 50% credit for first touch, 50% for last touch

**UTM Structure**:
```
utm_source: instagram, youtube, linkedin
utm_medium: influencer, organic, paid
utm_campaign: [influencer_name]_[month]_[content_type]
utm_content: [post_id or description]
```

**Example Link**:
```

```

**2. Tracking Dashboard Metrics**

**Influencer-Level Metrics**:
- **Reach**: Total impressions (follower count × post visibility)
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- **Click-Through Rate**: Link clicks / Reach
- **Conversion Rate**: Signups / Clicks
- **Cost Per Lead (CPL)**: (Fee + Commission) / Leads
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: (Fee + Commission) / Paying Customers
- **ROAS**: Revenue from influencer / Total influencer cost
- **Commission Earned**: Total payout to influencer

**Campaign-Level Metrics**:
- **Total Spend**: Sum of all influencer fees + commissions
- **Total Reach**: Sum of all influencer reach
- **Total Leads**: Sum of all leads generated
- **Total Revenue**: Sum of all revenue from influencer-sourced customers
- **Average CPL**: Total Spend / Total Leads
- **Average CPA**: Total Spend / Total Paying Customers
- **Overall ROAS**: Total Revenue / Total Spend

**Benchmark Targets**:
| Metric | Target | Acceptable | Poor |
|--------|--------|------------|------|
| Engagement Rate | 8%+ | 5-7% | <5% |
| CTR | 5%+ | 3-4% | <3% |
| Conversion Rate | 15%+ | 10-14% | <10% |
| CPL | <R$ 100 | R$ 100-150 | >R$ 150 |
| ROAS | 5x+ | 3-4x | <3x |

### Performance Optimization

**If Underperforming (ROAS <3x)**:

**Step 1: Diagnose the Issue**
- Low reach? → Influencer didn't post, algorithm issues
- Low engagement? → Content not resonating, wrong audience
- Low clicks? → Weak CTA, broken link
- Low conversions? → Wrong audience, landing page issues

**Step 2: Optimize Content**
- Test different hooks (pain vs. benefit vs. curiosity)
- Test different formats (Reel vs. carousel vs. static)
- Test different CTAs (link in bio vs. direct link vs. DM)
- Test different posting times

**Step 3: Optimize Funnel**
- Improve landing page (if low conversion)
- Add urgency (limited-time discount)
- Improve offer (more free trial, bonus content)
- Simplify signup process

**Step 4: Make Decision**
- Give 30 days to optimize
- If still underperforming → Pause or end partnership
- If improving → Continue with adjustments

---

**If Overperforming (ROAS >5x)**:

**Step 1: Scale Investment**
- Increase posting frequency (if influencer has capacity)
- Negotiate longer-term contract (better rates)
- Add performance bonuses (incentivize more)
- Expand to other platforms (if not already)

**Step 2: Leverage Success**
- Ask for testimonial (use in sales materials)
- Feature on ConectaPay website (social proof)
- Repurpose content (ads, email, website)
- Refer to other similar influencers (social proof)

**Step 3: Deepen Partnership**
- Invite to become brand ambassador (12-month contract)
- Offer revenue share (align long-term incentives)
- Co-create product features (influencer input)
- Host joint webinar (cross-promotion)

### Fraud Prevention

**Common Influencer Fraud Tactics**:

**1. Fake Followers**
- **Detection**: Sudden spikes in follower count, low engagement rate
- **Prevention**: Use tools like Social Blade, HypeAuditor to analyze
- **Action**: Require audience quality report before partnership

**2. Engagement Pods**
- **Detection**: Same accounts commenting on every post, generic comments
- **Prevention**: Monitor comment quality, look for real conversations
- **Action**: Not deal-breaker, but negotiate lower fee

**3. Fake Results**
- **Detection**: Tracking link shows no clicks but influencer claims otherwise
- **Prevention**: Use independent tracking (ConectaPay's own analytics)
- **Action**: Confront with data, pause payments until resolved

**4. Content Scraping**
- **Detection**: Influencer uses someone else's content as their own
- **Prevention**: Reverse image search before partnership
- **Action**: Immediate contract termination

## Part 11: Risk Mitigation

### Common Risks & Solutions

**Risk 1: Influencer Doesn't Deliver Content**
- **Prevention**: Clear deadlines in contract, penalty clauses
- **Solution**: Offer extension (1 week), then cancel partnership
- **Backup**: Always have backup influencers ready

**Risk 2: Low Performance (ROAS <2x)**
- **Prevention**: Thorough qualification process, start with small test
- **Solution**: Optimize content, adjust targeting, extend trial period
- **Backup**: Pause investment, redirect budget to top performers

**Risk 3: Brand Safety Issue (Influencer Scandal)**
- **Prevention**: Background research, brand safety scorecard
- **Solution**: Immediate contract termination, distance brand publicly
- **Backup**: Crisis communication plan prepared in advance

**Risk 4: Influencer Promotes Competitor**
- **Prevention**: Exclusivity clauses in contract (for Tiers 2-4)
- **Solution**: Legal action if contract breached
- **Backup**: Require takedown of competitor content

**Risk 5: Audience Backlash (Too Promotional)**
- **Prevention**: Content review before posting, maintain authenticity
- **Solution**: Acknowledge feedback, adjust approach, be transparent
- **Backup**: Pause campaign, reassess strategy
