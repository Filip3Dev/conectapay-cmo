# ConectaPay Partnership Strategy - Channel Sales & Integration Partners

## Executive Summary

**Objective**: Scale ConectaPay distribution through strategic partnerships with Brazilian SaaS providers, POS systems, and industry associations.

**Target**: 30-40% of total growth from partners by Year 2

**Partner Categories**:
1. **Technology Partners** (60%): SaaS integrations (POS, CRM, clinic/real estate software)
2. **Channel Partners** (30%): Agencies, consultants, resellers
3. **Strategic Partners** (10%): Industry associations, payment processors

**Economics**:
- Partner commission: 20-30% of first-year revenue (FYR)
- Customer discount: 10-15% (split margin with partner)
- Partner LTV: 5-10x acquisition cost
- Payback period: 3-6 months

---

**Criteria**:

## Commission Structure

### Standard Model: Revenue Share

| Partner Tier | Commission | Customer Discount | ConectaPay Retention |
|--------------|------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Strategic (Tier 1) | 30% of FYR | 15% off first year | 55% of FYR |
| Growth (Tier 2) | 25% of FYR | 12% off first year | 63% of FYR |
| Affiliate (Tier 3) | 20% of FYR | 10% off first year | 70% of FYR |

**Example Calculation** (Strategic Partner):
- Customer pays: R$ 300/month × 12 months = R$ 3.600
- 15% discount: R$ 3.600 × 0.85 = R$ 3.060 (customer pays)
- Partner commission: R$ 3.060 × 0.30 = R$ 918 (partner earns)
- ConectaPay revenue: R$ 3.060 × 0.70 = R$ 2.142 (we keep)

### Alternative Models

**1. Bounty Model (One-Time)**
- Better for: Agencies, consultants with one-off introductions
- Payout: R$ 500-1.000 per activated customer
- Condition: Customer pays first 3 invoices

**2. Hybrid Model (Bounty + Revenue Share)**
- Better for: High-value partners with ongoing relationships
- Payout: R$ 250 bounty + 15% revenue share
- Balances immediate reward with long-term alignment

**3. Volume Bonuses**
- 10+ customers/year: +5% commission
- 25+ customers/year: +10% commission
- 50+ customers/year: +15% commission + dedicated support

## Integration Requirements

### Technical Specifications

**1. API Integration (Clinic/Real Estate Software)**

**Required Endpoints**:
```javascript
// ConectaPay → Partner
GET /partner/customers/:id
// Fetch customer details (name, phone, appointments/leads)

POST /partner/webhooks/no-show
// Notify partner of detected no-show
// Body: { customer_id, appointment_time, recovery_attempted }

POST /partner/webhooks/recovery
// Notify partner of successful recovery
// Body: { customer_id, recovered_revenue, timestamp }

// Partner → ConectaPay
POST /conectapay/webhooks/appointment-created
// Trigger: New appointment booked
// Body: { customer_phone, customer_name, appointment_time }

POST /conectapay/webhooks/appointment-no-show
// Trigger: Appointment missed (no-show detected)
// Body: { customer_phone, customer_name, missed_time }

POST /conectapay/webhooks/lead-created
// Trigger: New lead in system (real estate)
// Body: { lead_phone, lead_name, property_interest }
```

**Authentication**:
- OAuth 2.0 (preferred)
- API keys with IP whitelisting (acceptable)
- Mutual TLS for enterprise partners

**Rate Limits**:
- 100 requests/minute per partner
- Burst allowance: 200 requests

**Data Privacy**:
- LGPD compliance required
- Customer consent for data sharing
- Data retention: 90 days max
- Right to deletion supported

## Fraud Prevention

### Partner Fraud Risks

**1. Self-Referral**
**Risk**: Partner refers themselves to earn commission
**Prevention**:
- Same IP/device detection
- Payment method matching
- Manual review for high-value self-referrals

**2. Commission Stacking**
**Risk**: Customer claims multiple referral codes
**Prevention**:
- Last-click attribution model
- One referral source per customer
- Audit trail for all referrals

**3. Fake Customers**
**Risk**: Partner creates fake accounts to earn commission
**Prevention**:
- Customer must pay first invoice (30-day hold)
- Churn monitoring (cancel within 90 days = clawback)
- Pattern detection (suspicious sign-up clusters)

**4. Discount Abuse**
**Risk**: Partner promotes discount publicly, diluting pricing
**Prevention**:
- Discount codes tied to specific partner
- Public discount monitoring
- Agreement terms prohibiting unauthorized promotion
