## Executive Summary

This document defines ConectaPay's complete sales process from first contact to closed deal. The process is designed to be **repeatable, scalable, and measurable**, with clear stage definitions, qualification criteria, and handoff processes.

**Key Design Principles**:
1. **Speed over perfection**: Fast response times beat perfect follow-ups
2. **Qualification first**: Disqualify early to save time
3. **ROI-driven messaging**: Every conversation reinforces value
4. **Data-informed**: Track everything, optimize based on results

# Part 1: Lead Qualification Framework

## ICP Definition

### Primary Segment: Clínicas Médicas

**Firmographics**:
- Industry: Clínicas médicas (all specialties)
- Size: 10-100 employees
- Revenue: R$ 50K-500K/month
- Appointments/week: 50+
- No-show rate: 15%+ (industry avg is 20-30%)
- Monthly loss from no-shows: R$ 2.000+

**Psychographics**:
- Pain threshold: R$ 2.000+/month loss
- Tech-savviness: Medium (uses WhatsApp, basic scheduling software)
- Automation openness: High (tired of manual tasks)
- Price sensitivity: Medium (ROI-focused, not cheapest)

**Decision Maker**:
- Role: Clinic Owner, Medical Director, or Office Manager
- Age: 30-55
- Goals: Reduce revenue loss, save staff time, improve operational efficiency
- Pain points: No-shows, staff wasted on calls, chaotic scheduling

---

### Secondary Segment: Imobiliárias

**Firmographics**:
- Industry: Real estate agencies
- Size: 10-50 agents
- Revenue: R$ 100K-1M/month
- Leads/week: 30+
- Conversion rate: <5%
- Monthly loss from poor follow-up: R$ 5.000+

**Decision Maker**:
- Role: Agency Owner or Sales Manager
- Goals: Increase lead conversion, reduce commission loss
- Pain points: Leads going cold, manual follow-up, inconsistent messaging

---

## BANT Qualification Framework

### 1. Budget (Orçamento)

**Target**: R$ 297-497/month

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | "Budget is available for ROI-positive tools" |
| 7 | "Need approval but likely within 30 days" |
| 4 | "Very tight budget, would need director approval" |
| 0 | "No budget, can't afford any tools" |

**Key Question**: "Você tem orçamento disponível para ferramentas que geram ROI no primeiro mês?"

---

### 2. Authority (Autoridade)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Decision maker (Owner, Director, Manager) |
| 7 | Influencer (can recommend to decision maker) |
| 4 | User (not decision maker) |
| 0 | No authority |

**Key Question**: "Quem aprova novas ferramentas na clínica/imobiliária?"

---

### 3. Need (Necessidade)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | "Losing R$ 2.000+/month to no-shows, urgent problem" |
| 7 | "Some no-shows, but not critical" |
| 4 | "Minor annoyance, not a priority" |
| 0 | "No problem, current solution works" |

**Key Questions**:
- "Quantos no-shows você tem por semana?"
- "Quanto isso custa por mês?"

---

### 4. Timeline (Prazo)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | "Need to solve this now" |
| 7 | "Looking to implement within 30 days" |
| 4 | "Evaluating options, no urgency" |
| 0 | "Not a priority, maybe next quarter" |

**Key Question**: "Quando você gostaria de começar a reduzir os no-shows?"

---

## Qualification Scoring System

### Lead Score Calculation

```
Total Score = Budget + Authority + Need + Timeline
Max Score = 40 points
```

### Lead Classification

| Classification | Score Range | Action |
|----------------|-------------|--------|
| **HOT LEAD** | 35-40 | Immediate call (within 2 hours), fast track to demo |
| **WARM LEAD** | 28-34 | Call within 24 hours, standard demo flow |
| **COLD LEAD** | 20-27 | Email nurture sequence, re-qualify in 30 days |
| **DISQUALIFY** | <20 | Add to general newsletter, no sales effort |

---

## Disqualification Criteria

**Immediately Disqualify If**:
- ❌ Monthly loss < R$ 1.000 (too small)
- ❌ Not using WhatsApp (technology blocker)
- ❌ "No-shows aren't a problem" (no pain)
- ❌ Looking for free solution (won't pay)
- ❌ One-person operation (too small)
- ❌ Already implementing similar solution (competition)

**Keep in Nurture If**:
- ⏸️ "Not right time, call me in 2 months" → Schedule follow-up
- ⏸️ "Need to talk to partner" → Send ROI materials, follow up in 1 week
- ⏸️ "Send me more information" → Add to email nurture, re-qualify in 2 weeks

# Part 2: Sales Stage Definition

## Complete Sales Pipeline (6 Stages)

```
LEAD → QUALIFIED → DISCOVERY → DEMO → PROPOSAL → CLOSED
```

---

## Stage 1: New Lead

**Definition**: Prospect enters the pipeline (ads, outreach, referrals)

**Entry Criteria**:
- Expresses interest (form fill, responds to outreach)
- Has valid contact info
- Matches basic ICP (clinic or real estate)

**Exit Criteria** (to Qualified):
- BANT score ≥28
- Confirmed pain (R$ 2.000+/month loss)
- Decision maker identified

**Activities**:
- Initial qualification call (15 min)
- BANT scoring
- CRM entry with basic data

**Target Duration**: <24 hours
**Conversion Goal**: 40% advance to Qualified

---

## Stage 2: Qualified

**Definition**: Lead meets BANT criteria, ready for discovery

**Entry Criteria**:
- BANT score ≥28
- Pain confirmed
- Decision maker identified

**Exit Criteria** (to Discovery):
- Discovery call scheduled
- Preliminary research completed (review their Instagram, website)

**Activities**:
- Schedule discovery call (30 min)
- Send pre-meeting materials (brief overview)
- Research prospect (Instagram, Google Maps reviews)

**Target Duration**: <3 days
**Conversion Goal**: 80% advance to Discovery

---

## Stage 3: Discovery

**Definition**: Deep needs assessment, relationship building

**Entry Criteria**:
- Discovery call scheduled

**Exit Criteria** (to Demo):
- Pain quantified (R$ X/month loss)
- Key stakeholders identified
- Budget confirmed (R$ 297-497 acceptable)
- Demo scheduled

**Activities**:
- Discovery call (30-45 min)
- ROI calculation (use calculator)
- Identify all stakeholders (who needs to approve?)
- Understand current process (how do you handle confirmations now?)
- Schedule demo with decision makers

**Target Duration**: 1 call
**Conversion Goal**: 60% advance to Demo

**Discovery Call Script**:

**Opening (5 min)**:
- "Obrigado pelo tempo. Me fale um pouco sobre sua clínica."
- "Como vocês gerenciam as confirmações de consulta hoje?"

**Pain Discovery (15 min)**:
- "Quantos no-shows vocês têm por semana?"
- "Quanto cada consulta não comparecida custa?"
- "Quanto tempo sua secretária gasta chamando pacientes?"
- "O que acontece quando alguém falta?"

**Solution Exploration (10 min)**:
- "Se você reduzisse no-shows em 70%, quanto recuperaria por mês?"
- "O que sua secretária faria com 3 horas livres por semana?"
- "Você já tentou alguma solução? O que funcionou/não funcionou?"

**Budget & Timeline (10 min)**:
- "Se tivéssemos uma solução que recuperasse R$ 3.000/mês por R$ 297/mês, você implementaria?"
- "Quem aprova esse tipo de ferramenta?"
- "Quando você gostaria de começar?"

**Closing (5 min)**:
- "Vou preparar uma demo personalizada para sua clínica. Podemos marcar [data/hora]?"
- "Vou enviar um calendário. Confirme, por favor."

---

## Stage 4: Demo

**Definition**: Product demonstration tailored to prospect's needs

**Entry Criteria**:
- Demo scheduled
- Key stakeholders invited
- Discovery insights documented

**Exit Criteria** (to Proposal):
- Demo completed
- Objections addressed
- Next step confirmed (proposal review)

**Activities**:
- Product demo (30 min)
- Live ROI calculation
- Q&A session
- Address specific objections
- Confirm next steps

**Target Duration**: 1 demo call
**Conversion Goal**: 50% advance to Proposal

**Demo Agenda**:

1. **Recap Discovery (5 min)**
   - "Baseado na nossa conversa, você perde R$ X/mês com no-shows"
   - "Vou mostrar como ConectaPay resolve exatamente esse problema"

2. **Problem-Solution Mapping (5 min)**
   - Show current process (manual, time-consuming)
   - Show ConectaPay process (automated, 5 min/day)

3. **Live Demo (15 min)**
   - Upload patient list
   - Configure automated reminders
   - Show real-time confirmations
   - Demonstrate Pix payment
   - Show analytics dashboard

4. **ROI Calculation (5 min)**
   - "Com seus números: 12 no-shows/semana × R$ 200 = R$ 2.400/mês"
   - "Reduzindo 70%: você recupera R$ 1.680/mês"
   - "Investimento: R$ 297/mês"
   - "ROI líquido: R$ 1.383/mês (4.7x)"

5. **Objection Handling (5 min)**
   - Address questions
   - Share testimonials from similar clinics

6. **Next Steps (5 min)**
   - "Vou enviar a proposta em 1 hora"
   - "Podemos revisar [data]?"

---

## Forecasting Methodology

### Simple Forecast (3-month view)

```
Forecast = (Pipeline Value × Weighted Probability) + (New MRR Expected)

Example:
- Pipeline: R$ 30.000
- Weighted Probability: 40%
- Expected New MRR: R$ 5.000

Forecast = (R$ 30.000 × 0.40) + R$ 5.000 = R$ 17.000
```
