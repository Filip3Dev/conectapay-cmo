## 📊 Current Messaging Assumptions vs. Validation Targets

### Assumption 1: No-Show Financial Impact
**Our Current Message**: "Perco R$ 2.500-R$ 5.000 por mês com no-shows"

**Validation Questions**:
- What is the ACTUAL monthly revenue loss?
- How do they currently calculate this loss?
- What's the average appointment value (ticket médio)?
- How many no-shows per week/month?
- Do they track this number or is it invisible?

**Customer Language to Capture**:
- [ ] Exact quote: "Perco cerca de ___ por mês"
- [ ] Exact quote: "Cada consulta vale ___"
- [ ] Exact quote: "Tenho ___ no-shows por semana"
- [ ] Calculation method they use
- [ ] Emotion words (frustrado, impotente, etc.)

**Validation Threshold**:
- ✅ VALIDATED if: 70%+ confirm R$ 2.000+ loss/month
- ⚠️ NEEDS REFINEMENT if: 50-70% confirm R$ 2.000+ loss/month
- ❌ INVALIDATED if: <50% confirm R$ 2.000+ loss/month

## 📈 Message Validation Scoring System

### Scoring Criteria (0-100 per message)

**Relevance Score (0-40 points)**:
- 40 pts: "This is EXACTLY my problem"
- 30 pts: "This is mostly my problem"
- 20 pts: "This is somewhat my problem"
- 10 pts: "This is rarely my problem"
- 0 pts: "This is not my problem"

**Accuracy Score (0-30 points)**:
- 30 pts: Numbers are exactly right
- 20 pts: Numbers are close
- 10 pts: Numbers are in the right ballpark
- 0 pts: Numbers are way off

**Resonance Score (0-30 points)**:
- 30 pts: Strong emotional reaction ("I need this now")
- 20 pts: Moderate interest ("This could work")
- 10 pts: Mild interest ("Maybe worth exploring")
- 0 pts: No reaction ("Doesn't matter to me")

### Message Quality Classification

**CLASS A (Keep & Scale)**: 80-100 points
- Use in all channels
- Lead with this message
- Test variations of it

**CLASS B (Refine & Test)**: 60-79 points
- Good foundation, needs tweaking
- Test specific elements (numbers, wording)
- Keep as secondary message

**CLASS C (Pivot or Kill)**: 0-59 points
- Major disconnect with reality
- Either completely reframe or stop using
- Replace with new message based on real data

## 🔄 Message Iteration Process

### After Every 5 Interviews

1. **Aggregate Scores**: Calculate average scores for each message
2. **Identify Patterns**: Find common language themes
3. **Extract Quotes**: Pull top 5 most powerful customer quotes
4. **Validate Assumptions**: Check if thresholds are met
5. **Draft New Variations**: Create 2-3 improved versions

### After 10 Interviews (Checkpoint)

1. **Full Validation Report**: Score all messages
2. **Classify Messages**: Label A/B/C for each
3. **Customer Language Bank**: Compile verbatim quotes
4. **Recommended Changes**: Specific messaging updates
5. **A/B Test Priorities**: Which messages to test first

### After 15-20 Interviews (Final)

1. **Final Validation**: All assumptions scored
2. **Messaging Update**: Comprehensive message refresh
3. **Copywriting Brief**: Updated language for all channels
4. **Smoke Test Recommendations**: Which messages to test in ads
5. **ICP Language Profile**: How our ICP actually talks about the problem

## 📝 Message Testing Protocol

### During Interviews: Message Reaction Testing

At the end of each interview, test 3 value props:

**Script**:
"Agora quero te mostrar 3 formas que outras clínicas têm descrito o problema. Me diga qual ressoa mais com você:"

**Option 1 (Pain-Focused)**:
"Sua clínica perde R$ 2.500-R$ 5.000 por mês com pacientes que não comparecem"

- Reaction: _____
- Quote: "________________"

**Option 2 (Time-Focused)**:
"Sua equipe gasta 3-5 horas por semana ligando para confirmar consultas que mesmo assim não são confirmadas"

- Reaction: _____
- Quote: "________________"

**Option 3 (Solution-Focused)**:
"Automatize lembretes e recupere 70% dos no-shows em 5 minutos por dia"

- Reaction: _____
- Quote: "________________"

**Follow-up Questions**:
- "Por que essa opção ressoou mais?"
- "Como você mudaria essa frase para soar mais verdadeira?"
- "Qual palavra você usaria para descrever isso?"

## 🎯 Output: Updated Messaging Framework

### After Validation Complete

Create updated messaging with:

**Headlines**: Use exact customer language
- Instead of: "Perca menos dinheiro com no-shows"
- Use: "Pare de perder R$ 3.200 por mês com pacientes que marcam e não aparecem"

**Subheadlines**: Use actual numbers from interviews
- Instead of: "Reduza no-shows em até 70%"
- Use: "De 12 no-shows por semana para 3 - assim como a Clínica [Nome]"

**Bullets**: Use verbatim customer words
- Instead of: "Economize tempo da sua equipe"
- Use: "Sua secretária volta a focar em atendimento, não em ligações repetidas"

**Social Proof**: Use real customer stories
- Instead of: "Clínicas como a sua recuperam R$ 3.200/mês"
- Use: "A Clínica DermatoEssência em São recuperou R$ 1.800 no primeiro mês"

**Objections**: Use actual objections heard
- Instead of: "Não funciona com meu sistema"
- Use: "Não sei se integra com o ClinicWeb que eu uso"
