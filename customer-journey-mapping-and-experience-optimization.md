## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive customer journey maps for ConectaPay across all target segments, identifying every touchpoint, pain point, and optimization opportunity from first awareness to long-term advocacy.

**Purpose**: Create a complete, actionable view of the customer experience to:
1. Identify friction points that cause drop-off
2. Prioritize optimization efforts by impact
3. Align all teams around the customer experience
4. Measure and improve journey performance over time

**Key Insights**:
- **Average journey length**: 47 days from awareness to activation
- **Critical drop-off points**: 3 major friction areas identified
- **Biggest opportunity**: Reducing time-to-first-value from 7 days to 48 hours
- **Revenue at stake**: R$ 180K/year in recoverable churn

# Part 2: Primary Segment Journey - Clínicas Médicas

## Complete Journey Map

### Stage 1: Awareness

**Duration**: Instant - 3 days
**Touchpoints**: 7
**Pain Score**: 2/10 (Low friction)
**Drop-off Rate**: 85%

#### Customer Mindset
- **Emotional State**: Frustrated, overwhelmed, skeptical
- **Primary Thought**: "Another day, another no-show. There must be a better way."
- **Motivation**: Revenue loss is becoming unbearable
- **Barriers**: "I've tried solutions before, they never work"

#### Touchpoint Inventory

| Touchpoint | Channel | Frequency | Owner | CX Score | Notes |
|------------|---------|-----------|-------|----------|-------|
| Meta Ads (Instagram/Facebook) | Paid | Daily | Marketing | 7/10 | Pain-driven creative resonates |
| Google Search Ads | Paid | Always on | Marketing | 8/10 | High intent, good quality |
| Instagram Organic Content | Organic | 3x/week | Marketing | 6/10 | Building awareness, low conversion |
| LinkedIn Posts | Organic | 2x/week | Marketing | 5/10 | Less relevant for clinics |
| WhatsApp Messages (Direct Outreach) | Direct | 1x | Sales | 9/10 | High personalization, best response |
| Referral from Peer | Word of Mouth | Ongoing | Product | 10/10 | Highest trust, fastest conversion |
| Google Maps Listing | Organic | Passive | Marketing | 4/10 | Low visibility, needs optimization |

#### Pain Points

1. **Ad Fatigue** (Severity: 6/10)
   - **Problem**: Clinics see many ads, tune them out
   - **Impact**: 45% of ads ignored
   - **Symptoms**: Low CTR (1.8%), high frequency needed
   - **Customer Quote**: "I skip all ads automatically"

2. **Skepticism** (Severity: 7/10)
   - **Problem**: "Another automation solution that won't work"
   - **Impact**: 60% don't click after seeing headline
   - **Root Cause**: Past bad experiences with overpromising tools
   - **Customer Quote**: "They all say the same thing"

3. **Timing Mismatch** (Severity: 5/10)
   - **Problem**: Ads shown when owner is busy with patients
   - **Impact**: 30% scroll past without reading
   - **Symptoms**: High impression count, low engagement

#### Optimization Strategies

**Priority 1: Reduce Skepticism**
- **Action**: Add social proof to ad creative (before/after metrics)
- **Implementation**: Testimonial video clips in first 3 seconds
- **Expected Impact**: +25% CTR, +15% conversion
- **Effort**: Medium (creative production)
- **Timeline**: 2 weeks

**Priority 2: Improve Ad Relevance**
- **Action**: Geographic + specialty targeting
- **Implementation**: Create 5 specialty-specific ad sets (dermatology, dentistry, etc.)
- **Expected Impact**: +35% relevance score, lower CPC
- **Effort**: Low (audience configuration)
- **Timeline**: 1 week

**Priority 3: Optimize Timing**
- **Action**: Schedule ads for clinic off-hours (7-9 AM, 6-8 PM)
- **Implementation**: Dayparting in Meta Ads
- **Expected Impact**: +20% engagement
- **Effort**: Low (campaign settings)
- **Timeline**: 2 days

#### Success Metrics

- **CTR**: 1.8% → 2.5% (+39%)
- **Link Click-Through Rate**: 3% → 4.5% (+50%)
- **Cost per Click**: R$ 2.50 → R$ 2.00 (-20%)
- **Awareness-to-Lead**: 3% → 5% (+67%)

# Part 4: Cross-Cutting Journey Issues

## Issue 1: The "Grand Canyon" Drop-off

**Location**: Between Consideration and Decision
**Severity**: Critical
**Impact**: 55% drop-off, R$ 600K/year in lost revenue

### The Problem

Customers get interested, do research, even attend demos... then go silent. We never hear from 55% of them again.

### Root Causes

1. **No urgency to decide**: "I can always do this next month"
2. **Fear of implementation**: "This seems complicated"
3. **Competitor confusion**: "Someone else offered me something similar"
4. **Budget timing**: "Not in this quarter's budget"

### Solutions

**Immediate Actions (Week 1)**:
- Add urgency: "This pricing ends Friday" (legitimate scarcity)
- Simplify: "We handle setup for you" message
- differentiate: "Unlike X, we guarantee results"
- flexible: "Start now, pay in 30 days"

**Long-term Fixes (Month 1)**:
- Implement "soft close" process: automated follow-up sequence
- Create "decision kit": comparison sheet, ROI calculator, implementation plan
- Train sales team on objection handling
- A/B test urgency vs. reassurance messaging

**Expected Impact**: +30% conversion, +R$ 180K/year

---

## Issue 2: The "Implementation Wall"

**Location**: Early Onboarding (Days 1-7)
**Severity**: Critical
**Impact**: 20% pre-activation churn, R$ 120K/year in lost revenue

### The Problem

Customers sign up, get stuck on WhatsApp Business verification, and never complete setup. This is our #1 churn point.

### Root Causes

1. **External dependency**: Meta's verification process is complex and slow
2. **No guidance**: Customers don't know what to do or expect
3. **Time constraints**: Clinic owners are busy, can't dedicate 2-3 hours
4. **Technical anxiety**: "I'm not good with technology"

### Solutions

**Immediate Actions (Week 1)**:
- Create "WhatsApp Setup Guide" video (2 minutes)
- Add "What to Expect" timeline graphic
- Implement proactive check-ins (Day 1, 3, 5)
- Add WhatsApp support button for immediate help

**Long-term Fixes (Month 1)**:
- **White-Glove Setup Service**: Dedicated CS agent handles entire process
- Pre-verification: Start verification before customer signs
- Alternative setup: "We'll set up a test number for you"
- Done-for-you: "Send us your documents, we'll handle it"

**Expected Impact**: -90% setup friction, +35% activation, +R$ 200K/year

---

## Issue 3: The "Value Gap"

**Location**: Onboarding → Activation (Days 7-14)
**Severity**: High
**Impact**: 30% question value, 10% churn

### The Problem

Customers complete setup but don't see results for 7+ days. They lose confidence, question the purchase, some churn.

### Root Causes

1. **Time to first automation**: 48 hours to build first automation
2. **Time to first recovery**: 7+ days to see first abandoned sale recovered
3. **No progress visibility**: Don't know if it's working
4. **Unclear attribution**: "Would this have happened anyway?"

### Solutions

**Immediate Actions (Week 1)**:
- Pre-load historical no-shows: Start working immediately
- Progress dashboard: "ConectaPay has sent 47 reminders, 12 responses"
- Attribution labeling: "Recovered by ConectaPay" on every success
- Daily updates: "Day 3: 23 reminders sent, 5 appointments saved"

**Long-term Fixes (Month 1)**:
- **Fast-Track Value**: Import last 30 days of no-shows, automate immediately
- Early wins: Identify "easy wins" (recent no-shows) to target first
- Celebration: High-touch celebration of first recovery (email, WhatsApp, maybe even phone call)
- Expectation setting: "Most see first recovery in 48-72 hours"

**Expected Impact**: First recovery in 48 hours, +50% activation, +R$ 150K/year

# Part 11: Journey Playbook

## Emergency Response Playbook

### Scenario 1: Sudden Drop-off Spike

**Trigger**: Drop-off rate increases >20% week-over-week at any stage

**Immediate Actions (Within 1 hour)**:
1. Alert stage owner + CX head
2. Investigate: Check for technical issues, recent changes, external factors
3. Customer outreach: Contact 5 customers who dropped off for feedback
4. Hypothesis: Create 2-3 potential causes

**Same-Day Actions**:
1. Implement quick fix if root cause identified
2. Create mitigation if root cause unclear
3. Communicate with affected customers
4. Document learnings

**Post-Mortem (Within 1 week)**:
1. Root cause analysis
2. Preventive measures
3. Process updates
4. Team training

### Scenario 2: Negative NPS Spike

**Trigger**: NPS drops >10 points month-over-month

**Immediate Actions**:
1. Review all customer feedback from period
2. Identify common themes
3. Reach out to detractors for deeper understanding
4. Create action plan

**Same-Week Actions**:
1. Implement top 2 fixes
2. Communicate changes to affected customers
3. Monitor NPS daily

### Scenario 3: Churn Spike

**Trigger**: Monthly churn exceeds 8% (target: 5%)

**Immediate Actions**:
1. Identify all at-risk customers (health score <50)
2. Reach out personally to understand reasons
3. Create retention offers where appropriate
4. Analyze patterns (segment, stage, usage)

**Same-Week Actions**:
1. Address systemic issues if identified
2. Improve health scoring accuracy
3. Implement early warning system
4. Create retention playbooks

## Appendix C: Metrics Definitions

### Time-to-First-Value (TTFV)
**Definition**: Number of days from contract signature to first recovered sale
**Formula**: Date of first recovery - Date of contract signature
**Target**: ≤2 days
**Current**: 7 days

### Activation Rate
**Definition**: Percentage of customers who achieve first value within 30 days
**Formula**: (Customers with first recovery within 30 days / Total customers) × 100
**Target**: ≥85%
**Current**: 65%

### Journey NPS
**Definition**: Net Promoter Score measured at Day 30 of journey
**Formula**: % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
**Target**: ≥65
**Current**: 45

### Stage Drop-off Rate
**Definition**: Percentage of customers lost between two consecutive stages
**Formula**: (Customers entering stage - Customers completing stage) / Customers entering stage × 100
**Target**: ≤35%
**Current**: 55%
